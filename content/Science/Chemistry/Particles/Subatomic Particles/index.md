- subatomic particles are [[Particles|particles]] much smaller than [[Atoms Elements|atoms]]

	- <strong>elementary/</strong><strong>fundamental particle</strong> - a subatomic particle with no sub structure, thus not composed of other particles

# Subatomic Particles - Breakdown

<strong>[Elementary/Fundamental Particles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_particles#Elementary_particles)</strong> - subatomic particle with no sub structure, thus not composed of other particles
- <strong>[Fermions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_particles#Fermions)</strong> - a particle that follows [Fermi–Dirac statistics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fermi%E2%80%93Dirac_statistics), obeys the [Pauli exclusion principle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pauli_exclusion_principle), and have half-integer spin. building blocks of matter
	- <strong>[Quarks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_particles#Quarks)</strong> -  interact via the [strong nuclear interaction](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strong_interaction)
	- <strong>[Leptons](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_particles#Leptons)</strong> - does not interact via the [strong nuclear interaction](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strong_interaction)
- <strong>[Bosons](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_particles#Bosons)</strong> - a particle that follows [Bose–Einstein statistics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bose%E2%80%93Einstein_statistics) and all have integer spins. also called force carrier particles which give rise to [[Force]]

<strong>[Composite Particles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_particles#Composite_particles)</strong> - subatomic particle that is made of more than one quark
- <strong>[Hadrons](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_particles#Hadrons)</strong> - particles composed solely of quarks or both quarks and antiquarks
	- <strong>[Baryons](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_particles#Baryons)</strong> - composite particles made of three quarks
	- <strong>[Mesons](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_particles#Mesons)</strong> - unstable subatomic particles composed of one quark and one antiquark

All the known matter particles are composites of fermions (quarks and leptons), and they interact by exchanging bosons (force carrier particles)
