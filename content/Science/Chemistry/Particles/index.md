In [[Chemistry|chemistry]] and [physical sciences](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Outline_of_physical_science), a <strong>particle</strong> is a small localizedobject to which can be ascribed several physical or chemical properties such as volume, density or mass.

### Particle Sizes

- <strong>[[Subatomic Particles|subatomic particles]]</strong> like the [[Electron|electrons]]
- <strong>[microscopic particles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microscopic_scale)</strong> like [[Atoms Elements|atoms]], [[Molecules - Compounds|molecules, and compounds]]
- <strong>[macroscopic particles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Macroscopic_scale)</strong> like [powders](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Powder_\(substance\)) and other [granular materials](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Granular_material)
