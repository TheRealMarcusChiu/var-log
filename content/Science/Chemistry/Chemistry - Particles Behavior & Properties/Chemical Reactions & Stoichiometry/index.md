- <strong>molecule</strong> - a group of same atoms bonded together
- <strong>compound</strong> - a group of different atoms bonded together

# Chemical Reactions

chemical reactions are often described with an equation of the format below

- reactants → products
- 2H<sub>2</sub> + O<sub>2</sub> → 2H<sub>2</sub>O

chemical reactions can be either:

- reversible - where products can easily become its original reactant form
- irreversible - where products requires lots of energy to become its original reactant form

# Stoichiometry

involves using relationships between reactants and/or products in a chemical reaction to determine desired quantitative data 

more on stoichiometry - <https://www.khanacademy.org/science/chemistry/chemical-reactions-stoichiome/stoichiometry-ideal/a/stoichiometry>
