<strong>Chemistry</strong>
- is the [[Science|scientific]] discipline involving[[Atoms Elements|atoms]], [[Molecules - Compounds|molecules, and compounds]]: their composition, structure, properties, behavior and the changes they undergo during a reaction with other substances
- occupies an intermediate position between [[Physics|physics]] and [[Biology|biology]]
