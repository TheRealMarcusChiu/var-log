- multipotential hematopoietic stem cell - is a type of [[(Multi／Pluri／Toti) Potential Stem Cells|multipotential stem cell]]

    - small lymphocyte
      - B lymphocyte
        - plasma cell
      - T lymphocyte
    - natural killer (NK) cell (large granular lymphocyte)
  - common myeloid progenitor
    - megakaryocyte
      - thrombocytes - another term for platelets
    - erythrocyte - red blood cell
    - mastocyte or labrocyte or mast cell -
    - myeloblast
      - basophil
      - neutrophil
      - eosinophil
      - monocyte - in bloodstream
        - macrophage - a monocyte that left the bloodstream and becomes part of tissue
