### Parts of Brain
- brain stem
- cerebellum
- cerebrum

### Parts of Cerebrum
- is divided into 2 hemispheres by longitude fissure
- the hemispheres are connected by:
	- corpus callosum - larger
	- commissures - smaller
- surrounded by cerebral cortex

### Parts of Cerebral Cortex
- frontal lobe
- parietal lobe
- temporal lobe
- occipital lobe

### Difference Between Cerebrum & Cerebral Cortex
- <strong>Cerebrum</strong>
	- the most anterior of the vertebrate brain
	- made up of both gray and white matter
	- comprises both cell bodies and nerve fibers
	- composed of 2 hemispheres
	- major function is to control voluntary muscular movements of body
- <strong>Cerebral Cortex</strong>
	- outer layer of cerebrum
	- made up of gray matter
	- comprises cell bodies and dendrites
	- composed of 4 lobes
	- mainly involved in consciousness
