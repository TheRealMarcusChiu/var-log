<strong>bacterium</strong> - <strong>bacteria</strong> - <strong>microbes</strong>
- a member of a large group of unicellular microorganisms which have cell walls but lack organelles and an organized nucleus, including some which can cause disease

<strong>bacteriophage</strong> - <strong>phage</strong> - <strong>virus</strong>
- a virus that parasitizes a bacterium by infecting it and reproducing inside it

<strong>microbiome</strong>
- the microorganisms in a particular environment (including the body or a part of the body)
