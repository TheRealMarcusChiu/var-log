###### Ampere-Hour (Ah)
````excerpt
- having dimensions of electric current (A) multiplied by time (h)
- equal to the charge transferred by a steady current of one ampere flowing for one hour, or 3,600 [[Coulomb (C)|coulombs]]
````
^excerpt
