###### Ampere (Amp)
````excerpt
- is the [[Metric System - International System of Units (SI Units)|SI]] base unit of electrical current
- one ampere (𝐴) is equal to 1 coulomb (𝐶) or 6.241509074×10¹⁸ electrons worth of charge moving past a point in a second (𝑠):
	- 𝐴 = 𝐶/𝑠
````
^excerpt
