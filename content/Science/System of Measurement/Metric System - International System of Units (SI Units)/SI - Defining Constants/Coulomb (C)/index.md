###### Coulomb (C)
````excerpt
- is the standard unit of electric charge in the [[Metric System - International System of Units (SI Units)|International System of Units (SI)]]
- the quantity of 1 coulomb is equal to the electrical charge of approximately 6.24 x 10<sup>18</sup> electrons or protons
- it is the amount of electricity that a 1-[[Ampere (Amp)|ampere (A)]] current carries in one second (s)
````
