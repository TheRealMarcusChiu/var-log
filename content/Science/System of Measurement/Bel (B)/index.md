###### Bel (B)
````excerpt
- 1 unit of bel represented the ratio of 10 to 1 (Thus a power ratio of 10:1 = 1 bel, 100:1 = 2 bels, and 1000:1 = 3 bels)
- was named after Alexander Graham Bell
````
^excerpt

# B - Formula

The bel of 𝑃<sub>1</sub> in reference to 𝑃<sub>2</sub> is defined by the following formula:
- $B = \log_{10}(\dfrac{P_1}{P_2})$
