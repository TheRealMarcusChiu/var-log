> [!expand]- Autism vs Down Syndrome
> down syndrome and autism are entirely different
>
> ###### Autism
>
> cause is unknown
>
> three main clusters of symptoms recognised as important for diagnosis:
>
> - social impairments
> - communication impairments
> - repetitive stereotyped behaviors
>
> A diagnosis of autism requires significant impairment in all three areas (see ICD-10 criteria in box) but a child who shows some but not all the difficulties may be described as having autistic spectrum disorder (ASD) not full autism
>
> ###### Down Syndrome
>
> cause - genetic condition called trisomy 21, where a person inherits an extra copy of chromosome 21

> [!expand]- Causes Depression
> Combinations Of:
>
> - imbalance of brain chemicals
> - faulty mood regulation by the brain
> - genetic vulnerability
> - stressful life events
> - medications
> - medical problems
>
> identified genes that make individuals more vulnerable to low moods
>
> more important than levels of specific brain chemical are:
>
> - nerve cell connections
> - nerve cell growth
> - functioning of nerve circuits
