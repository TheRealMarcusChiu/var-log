three basic models of observational learning:

1. <strong>live model</strong> - involves an actual individual demonstrating or acting out a behavior
2. <strong>verbal instructional model </strong>- involves descriptions and explanations of a behavior
3. <strong>symbolic model</strong> - involves real or fictional characters displaying behaviors in books, films, television programs, or online media
