###### Theory of Affordance

- a term coined by psychologist James J. Gibson
- states that when intelligent beings look at the world they perceive not simply objects and their relationships but also their possibilities. In other words, the chair “affords” the possibility of sitting. The water “affords” the possibility of swimming
