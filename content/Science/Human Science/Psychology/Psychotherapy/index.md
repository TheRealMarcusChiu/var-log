<strong>psychotherapy</strong> is a general term that is used to describe the process of treating [psychological disorders](https://www.verywellmind.com/what-is-a-psychological-disorder-2795767) and mental distress by the use of verbal and psychological techniques

### Formats of Psychotherapy
- <strong>Individual therapy</strong> - which involves working one-on-one with a psychotherapist
- <strong>Couples therapy</strong> - which involves a therapist working with a couple to help improve how the two function in their relationship
- <strong>Family therapy</strong> - which centers on improving the dynamic within families and can include multiple individuals within a family unit
- <strong>Group therapy</strong> - which involves a small group of individuals who share a common goal. This approach allows members of the group to offer and receive support from others, as well as practice new behaviors within a supportive and receptive group.
