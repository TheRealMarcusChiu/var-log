###### Learning Disability/Disorder/Difficulty
- is a condition in the brain that causes difficulties comprehending or processing information and can be caused by several different factors

# Learning Disability/Disorder/Difficulty - Types
- <strong>dyslexia</strong> - difficulty in reading
- <strong>dysgraphia</strong> - difficulty in writing
- <strong>dyscalculia</strong> - difficulty in arithmetic

# Language Disability/Disorder/Difficulty - Types
- <strong>dysphasia </strong>vs<strong> aphasia</strong> - difficulty in understanding and speaking. is a language disorder
	- aphasia is a full loss of language
	- dysphasia is partial loss of language
- receptive
- expressive
