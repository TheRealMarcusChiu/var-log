<strong>Spatial Visual Reasoning</strong> is the ability to visualize special patterns and mentally manipulate figures

<strong>Spatial-Temporal Reasoning</strong> is the ability to visualize special patterns and mentally manipulate them over a time-ordered sequence of spatial transformations
