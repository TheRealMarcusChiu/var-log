- a <strong>first principle</strong> is a foundational proposition or assumption that stands alone
- we cannot deduce <strong>first principles</strong> from any other proposition or assumption
- Aristotle, writing on <strong>first principles</strong>, said: ... Reasoning by <strong>first principles</strong> removes the impurity of assumptions and conventions
