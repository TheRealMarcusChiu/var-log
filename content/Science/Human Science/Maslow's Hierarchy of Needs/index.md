<strong>Maslow's <font style="color: rgb(82,86,90);">hierarchy of needs</font></strong> is a motivational theory in [[Psychology|psychology]] comprising a five-tier model of human <font style="color: rgb(82,86,90);">needs</font>, often depicted as <font style="color: rgb(82,86,90);">hierarchical</font> levels within a pyramid. From the bottom of the <font style="color: rgb(82,86,90);">hierarchy</font> upwards, the <font style="color: rgb(82,86,90);">needs</font> are: physiological, safety, love and belonging, esteem, and self-actualization
