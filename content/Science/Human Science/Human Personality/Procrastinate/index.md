<strong>procrastinate</strong> - delay or postpone action; put off doing something

### Procrastinate - Why We Procrastinate

- not as rewarding
- boring
- afraid of failing
- TODO - <https://solvingprocrastination.com/why-people-procrastinate/>

people tend to procrastinate less when:

- the deadline is closer
- the task is broken up into small chunks, they feel like they've accomplished something every time they turn in one of those chunks
