how procrastination affected the view of future selves

people were asked to picture 3 things while their neurons are being monitored:

- future selves
- current selves
- some stranger

2 cases, when people pictured their future selves:

1. neurons were activated in a similar way to when they pictured their current selves (they felt a high continuity with who they would be in the future)
2. neurons were activated in a similar way to when they pictured some stranger (less future self continuity)

case 2 tended to procrastinate more
