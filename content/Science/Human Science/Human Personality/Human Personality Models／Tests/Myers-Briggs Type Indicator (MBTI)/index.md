<strong>Myers–Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI)</strong> is an introspective self-report questionnaire indicating differing psychological preferences in how people:
- perceive the world and make decisions

![[Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI)/MyersBriggsTypes.png|800]]
# 1 - [[Extraversion／Extroversion／Extravert／Extrovert - Introversion／Introvert|Extraversion vs Introversion]]
When getting your energy:
- do you prefer to focus on the outer world (extraversion)
- do you prefer to focus on the inner world (introversion)

# 2 - Sensing vs Intuition
When taking in information:
- do you prefer to focus on the raw information (sensing)
- do you prefer to focus on the interpretations and meanings (intuition)

# 3 - Thinking vs Feeling
When making decisions:
- do you prefer to first look at logic and consistency (thinking)
- do you prefer to first look at the people and special circumstances (feeling)

# 4 - Judging vs Perceiving

When living your life outwardly:
- do you prefer a more structured and decided lifestyle (judging - does not mean being judgmental)
- do you prefer a more flexible and adaptable lifestyle (perceiving - does not mean being perceptive)
