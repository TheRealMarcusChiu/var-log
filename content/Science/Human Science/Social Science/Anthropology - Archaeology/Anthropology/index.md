<strong>Anthropology</strong>
- study of humanity, concerned with human behavior, human biology, and societies, in both the present and past, including the past human species

# Sub-Divisions
- <strong>Archaeological Anthropology </strong>has been derived from the broad field of Archaeology concerning the study of the extinct cultures
- <strong>Social Anthropology</strong> – studies patterns of behavior
- <strong>Cultural Anthropology</strong> – studies cultural meaning, including norms and values
- <strong>Linguistic Anthropology</strong> – studies how language influences social life
- <strong>Biological/Physical Anthropology</strong> – studies biological and behavioral aspects of human beings, their related non-human primates, and their extinct hominin ancestors
- <strong>Philosophical Anthropology</strong> – studies what is it to be human
