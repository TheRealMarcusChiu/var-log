### Relationship Types
- <strong>polygamy</strong>
	- the practice of having more than one wife or husband at a time
	- <strong>polygyny</strong> - the practice of a MALE having multiple wives
	- <strong>polyandry</strong> - the practice of a FEMALE having multiple husbands
- <strong>polyamory</strong>
	- means <em>many loves</em>
	- the practice of having more than one open romantic relationships
- <strong>polyfidelity</strong>
	- the practice where members of group are considered equal and can engage in sexual activity only within the group
	- can either:
		- allow new members
		- disallow new members

- <strong>polycule</strong>
	- used to describe a set of connected non-monogamous relationships
