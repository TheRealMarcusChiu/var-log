- <strong>eros</strong> - erotic love involving physical attraction and emotional intensity
- <strong>agape</strong> - sacrificial love involving placing a loved one’s welfare above your own
- <strong>storge</strong> - familial love refers to natural or instinctual affection, such as the love of a parent towards offspring and vice versa
- <strong>philia</strong> - friendly love
- <strong>pragma</strong> - love as a “shopping list” of desired attributes, such as being a good parent
- <strong>ludus</strong> - love as a game
- <strong>mania</strong> - jealous, obsessive, dependent love
