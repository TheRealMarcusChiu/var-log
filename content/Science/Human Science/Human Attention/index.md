### Attention

<strong>biased competition</strong> - a rudimentary mechanism of attention: the selection neurons to be stimulated at the expense of others

for example, when there is just one object in environment there is no need for attention. therefore <strong>attention</strong> is the amount of competing information to which our brains are exposed that implies a choice
### Types of Attention
1. <strong>controlled attention</strong> - conscious focus on a particular thing within an environment
2. <strong>stimulus-driven attention</strong> - involuntary attraction to an unexpected event in a person's immediate environment
3. <strong>arousal attention</strong> - e.g. tiredness descends upon person
