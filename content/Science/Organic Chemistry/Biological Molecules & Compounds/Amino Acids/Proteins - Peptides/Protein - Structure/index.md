# 4 hierarchies of Protein Structures

- <strong>primary structure</strong> - order of [[Amino Acids|amino acids]]
- <strong>secondary structure</strong> - due to interactions of backbone
  - <strong>β-pleated sheet</strong>
    - <strong>parallel</strong>
    - <strong>anti-parallel</strong>
  - <strong>α-helix</strong>
- <strong>tertiary structure</strong> - due to interactions between the R groups of the amino acids that make up the protein
- <strong>quaternary structure</strong> - arrangement of multiple peptide chains
