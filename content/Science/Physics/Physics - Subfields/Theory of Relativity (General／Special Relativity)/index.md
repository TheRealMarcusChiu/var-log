<strong>Theory of Relativity </strong>encompasses two interrelated theories by [Albert Einstein](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Albert_Einstein):
- [special relativity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special_relativity) applies to all physical phenomena in the absence of gravity
- [general relativity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/General_relativity) explains the law of gravitation and its relation to other forces of nature
