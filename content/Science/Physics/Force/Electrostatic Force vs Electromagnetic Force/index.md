### Electrostatic Forces

Electrostatic forces are forces created exclusively by charges acting on each other

𝐅𝑒𝑠 = 𝑞𝐄

- <strong>𝐅𝑒𝑠</strong>
- <strong>E</strong> is electric field
- 𝑞 is charge

This works when only electric fields are present, and therefore all charger are moving slowly (moving a charge creates a magnetic field <strong>B</strong>, but if the movement is slow, the magnetic field is insignificant).

### Electromagnetic Forces

Electromagnetic forces are forces created by both electric and magnetic fields

𝐅𝑒𝑚 = 𝑞(𝐄+𝐯×𝐁)

- <strong>v</strong> is velocity of particle
- 𝑞 is the charge of particle
- 𝐁 is magnetic field
- 𝐄 is electric field
