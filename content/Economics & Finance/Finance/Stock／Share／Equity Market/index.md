###### Stock Market - Equity Market - Share Market
- is the aggregation of buyers and sellers of stocks (also called shares), which represent ownership claims on businesses, these may include securities listed in either a:
	- public stock exchange
	- equity crowdfunding platforms (private stock exchange)
