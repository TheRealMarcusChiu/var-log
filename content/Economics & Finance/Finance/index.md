###### Finance
- is the management of money, particularly in relation to companies, organizations, or governments
- specifically, it deals with the questions of how and why an individual, company, or government acquires the money needed - called [capital](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capital_(finance)) in the company context - and how they spend or [invest](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Investment) that money
- major categories:
	- [corporate finance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Corporate_finance)
	- [personal finance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Personal_finance)
	- [public finance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Public_finance)
