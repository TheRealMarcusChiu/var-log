###### Economics

- studies the production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services
- main areas of focus:
  - scarcity and its implications for the use of resources
  - production of goods and services
  - growth of production and welfare over time
- four key economic concepts that help explain the many decisions that humans make:
  - scarcity
  - the law of supply and demand
  - efficiency - costs and benefits
  - incentives
