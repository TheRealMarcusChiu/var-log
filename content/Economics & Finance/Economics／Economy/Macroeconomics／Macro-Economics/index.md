###### Macroeconomics/Macro-Economics
- is a branch of [[Economics／Economy|economics]] dealing with the performance, structure, behavior, and decision-making of an economy as a whole. For example, using interest rates, taxes, and government spending to regulate an economy’s growth and stability. This includes regional, national, and global economies
