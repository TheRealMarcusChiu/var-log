- <strong>official order </strong>- is given by a court a law telling someone that they must do something or stop doing something
- <strong>proclamation</strong> - a public or official announcement, especially one dealing with a matter of great importance
- <strong>mandate</strong> - an official order or commission to do something
- <strong>edict</strong> - an official order or proclamation issued by a person in authority
