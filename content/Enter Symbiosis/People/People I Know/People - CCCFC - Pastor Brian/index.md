# What Made You Decide to Become a Pastor?
- when growing up treated church as a set of rules and he liked following rules
- when in college started not going to church
- after 2 or 3 years a guy who is persistent in asking him to join a small group and Brian avoided him (too the point Brian knew exactly the right time when the guy would knock on his door in the morning - Brian would instruct his roommates to turn off all lights and ignore the knock)
- Brian finally gave in to that persistent guy and went to the church group
- he felt the joy of being back in community
- the local church was in need of youth leaders to teach the book of Genesis; and out of false pretense Brian volunteered. Unfortunately he didn't know much or felt like he didn't know much
- BUT Brian loved consuming it Genesis (Psalms 34:8 - taste and see that the Lord is good; blessed are those who take refuge in his word)
- Brian then came across Exodus 1:8 "Then a new king, to whom Joseph meant nothing, came to power in Egypt" before the new king Joseph was everything and second most powerful in Egypt behind the pharaoh. But in verse 8 Joseph became nothing.
	- Brian found his purpose through this verse that: generations come and generations go
- after college in Florida he went to Dallas for seminary school, and the rest is history
