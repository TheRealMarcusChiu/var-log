- The Trolley Problem
- Would you kill an innocent person and immediately forget about it for the rest of your life; or not kill an innocent person and constantly think that you killed an innocent person for the rest of your life?
- If you could eliminate any crime - but you will have to commit that crime. what would it be?
- Let's say you have 1 week left to live and you are working on finishing a project of some kind (e.g. art, music, etc) You are told that a meteorite would hit earth a week from now and everyone on earth will die. Would you finish your project? Would you feel less motivated? Maybe the reverse corollary of this, part of the reason why you are working on a project is BECAUSE it will outlive you; and because it's an exercise to outlive your own death. Maybe perhaps that's what motivating ever human behavior. If that's the case, then if we were to remove this fear of death, NO ONE DOES ANYTHING. No one write books, no one paints paintings, etc; because there isn't that motivation anymore.
