Plastic recycling symbols does not denote recyclability but the plastic type

# Plastic Recycling Symbol #1: PET or PETE

PET (also abbreviated PETE) is short for polyethylene terephthalate

# Plastic Recycling Symbol #2: HDPE

High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE)

# Plastic Recycling Symbol #3: PVC

Polyvinyl chloride

# Plastic Recycling Symbol #4: LDPE

Low-density polyethylene (LDPE)

# Plastic Recycling Symbol #5: PP

Polypropylene

# Plastic Recycling Symbol #6: PS

Polystyrene

# Plastic Recycling Symbol #7: OTHER

A wide variety of plastics that don’t fit into the previously numbered categories are lumped into this one
