PREP is a formula for impromptu speaking
- start by stating my <strong>Proposition</strong>
- go on to explain the<strong> Reasons</strong> for this proposition
- give one or more<strong> Examples</strong> that illustrate my position
- then finish by either:
	- re-stating my <strong>Proposition</strong>
	- making a new <strong>Proposition</strong> for further action
