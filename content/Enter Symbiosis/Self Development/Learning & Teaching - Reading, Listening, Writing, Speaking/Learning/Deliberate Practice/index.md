The rule, 10,000 hours to mastery is false. A doctor of 20 year experience performs no better than a doctor of 5 years. This is because most doctors have accepted their performance as good enough.

What we need is <em>Deliberate Practice</em>

Deliberate Practice = Purposeful Practice Methods + Expert Coaching

Components of 'Purposeful Practice'
- specific goal
- intense focus
- immediate feedback
- frequent discomfort - dwelling between what you can and can not do
