###### critical theory (lower case)
````excerpt
- is an approach to [[Social Philosophy|social philosophy]] that focuses on society and culture to attempt to reveal, critique, and challenge [power structures](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Power_structure)
- it argues that:
	- [social problems](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Social_problems) stem more from [social structures](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Social_structure) and [cultural assumptions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cultural_assumptions) than from individuals
	- [ideology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ideology) is the principal obstacle to human liberation
````
^excerpt
