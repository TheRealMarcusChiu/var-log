"Theologically distinct (yet practically inseparable) from baptism of the Holy Spirit, water baptism is directly related to receiving and believing the Gospel, repenting, being forgiven, cleansed, and saved from a life of sin, calling on, confessing, and converting to Christ, making a public pledge of covenant commitment and incorporation into the visible expression of the Body of Christ, and commencing a life of discipleship" ~ Michael Svigel
