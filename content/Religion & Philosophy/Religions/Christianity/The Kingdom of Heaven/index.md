<em>kingdom</em>; the realm in which a king sovereignly rules. A <em>kingdom</em> always requires a <em>king</em> – as the kingdom of God does with <em>King Jesus</em>! especially refers to the rule of Christ in the hearts of believers – which is a rule that "one day will be universal on the physical earth in the Millennium"
