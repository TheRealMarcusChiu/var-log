- the Law - the commands from God, the Word of God
- prophets - speaks of the fore-coming Son. they are the spokespersons of God (Hebrews 1:1)
- Jesus
  - is the Word incarnated. John 1:14
  - fulfills the Law and Prophets. Matthew 5:17
- Holy Spirit - testifies the Son
  - John 15:26 - the Advocate testifies
  - Matthew 16:17 - Jesus replied, “Blessed are you, Simon son of Jonah, for this was not revealed to you by flesh and blood, but by my Father in heaven.
  - 1 Corinthians 2:4-5 - our faith rests on the power of the Holy Spirit
  - Romans 12:3 - our faith always comes from God

### Law - Prophets - Jesus - Holy Spirit

all 4 originates/comes from God

all 4 is life giving

- Jesus's body has been broken down (Bread of Life)
  - Hebrews 9:22 - without the shedding of blood there is no forgiveness

  - Communion is the exchange of thoughts and feelings especially at a spiritual and mental level
