# Dependence on God
- it doesn't mean pretending you're okay
- it doesn't mean polished faith, but honest connection
- it is not a feeling, but a decision you revisit daily
- loneliness and heartbreak are not signs you're failing spiritually

It means Telling the Truth about what you're feeling
# Prayer

God, I don’t feel okay right now.
I feel lonely, and I miss what I had.
I keep replaying things I wish I did differently.
Part of me feels like I wasn’t enough.
I don’t fully feel your love right now, but I want to trust that it’s still there.
Help me not to define myself by this loss.
Help me heal honestly, not just quickly.
Stay with me in this—even if I don’t feel it.
# Breakups and Unrequited love

This hits deeper, what's gone is not just the person, but also
- the version of yourself you felt when you were with them
- and the future you that you've imagined being gone

So when it doesn't work out, your mind tries to explain it:
- "what's wrong with me?"
- "why wasn't I enough?"
- "Did I mess up?"

Here's an important correction:
- A relationship not working != you being unworthy of love

# Belief in Eternal Life isn't Just About the Afterlife

It also reshapes how you see this life.

Your life doesn't just end here

In Psalm 22
- it goes from: despair → to hope → to restoration
- the structure itself is the message

Pain is real, but it is not the endpoint
