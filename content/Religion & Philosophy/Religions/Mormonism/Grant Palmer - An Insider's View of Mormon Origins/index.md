<strong>An Insider's View of Mormon Origins</strong> is a 2002 book about the origins of [[Mormonism]] by [Grant H. Palmer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grant_H._Palmer), a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS Church) who is a retired Church Educational System instructor and Institute director with a master's degree in history
