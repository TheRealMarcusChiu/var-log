in response to a struggling friend

Dear Heavenly Father, I pray that you give us pure of mind as we speak in love and in faithfulness. in spirit and in truth.

There are times when we read the Bible and it just doesn't sit well with us. We just don't flatout agree with it. For there are things in the Bible and what Paul had said that are hard to hear and be obedient to.

Father I pray for one thing: that we know that Your Word is Good.

Let us know that Paul was called to be an Apostle of Jesus Christ by THE WILL OF GOD. For God appointed him to know his will, and to see the Righteous One, and to hear His voice. For Paul once stood against Christ but is now with Christ. He grieves those who do not know You and he even wishes to exchange places with them.

Father let us trust in You with all our heart and lean not on our own understanding; in all our ways submit to Him, and thus You will make our paths straight. For thy Word is the lamp to our feet, and the light to our path. It doesn't make sense to march around the walls of Jericho, but let us has faith in Your Word for Your Word never fails, and endures forever.

Lord, you are the vinedresser and we are the branches. you cut off those who don't bear fruit and prune those who bear fruit so they could produce even more fruit. The pruning hurts, it is just temporary, but we know it is good for it comes from the Lord. There are times when life gets hard: there's too much to do, life seems to go down, we get spiritually attacked from all sides. But let us abide in You and You in us, for no branch can bear fruit by itself unless it abides in the vine, neither can we bear fruit unless we abide in You. Let us consider it pure joy my brothers and sisters whenever we face face trials of various kinds, tribulation and temptations of various kinds, because we know the testing of our faith produces perseverance and in the end we may be mature and complete, not lacking anything. For you are our shepard and we lack nothing. You are thy Word that gives us Light. Let us keep our eyes on what is Light. for when our eyes are on the Light, our body shall be full of light. But if our eyes are set on anything else, our bodies become full of darkness, how great is that darkness!

We pray that our love and faithfulness increase:

In faith so that we may be like Peter who walks on water as he kept his eyes on Jesus. But let us not stumble upon the waves which divert our eyes and thus sink our bodies. Even when we sink and fall I pray that we abide in you, feel our way towards You, and perhaps reach out for You.

We pray in ever increasing love for one another, as this is what was commanded. Let us abide in Your Name we pray amen.
