The Parable of the Sower

1. Path - hears but does not understand
2. Rocky Ground - external struggles
3. Thorns - internal struggles
4. Good soil - hears and understands

See, Hear, Understand Eyes, ears, heart

The Parable of the Weeds

While everyone was sleeping, the enemy came and sowed weeds among the wheat, and went away.

Be on guard. Armor of God Ephesians

The Parable of the Mustard Seed

"The kingdom of heaven is like a mustard seed, which a man took and planted in his field. Though it is the smallest of all seeds, yet when it grows, it is the largest of garden plants and becomes a tree, so that the birds come and perch in its branches."

With the help of God, Francis Chan started a church in his living room. And the church grew to several thousands.

Just like the mustard seed it is small but it becomes a tree. It yields 100, 60, 30 times what was sown. This yield does not have to be a mega church or anything that we could see with our earthly eyes. It can be scattered across great distances. Physical and or social. But it all starts somewhere like a mustard seed.
