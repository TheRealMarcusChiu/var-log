Matthew 13 is one of the passages I go back to often. And I do not know why
1. The kingdom of heaven is like a man who sowed good seed in his field
2. The kingdom of heaven is like a mustard seed, which a man took and planted in his field
3. The kingdom of heaven is like yeast that a woman took and mixed into about sixty pounds of flour until it worked all through the dough
4. The kingdom of heaven is like treasure hidden in a field
5. Again, the kingdom of heaven is like a merchant looking for fine pearls
6. Once again, the kingdom of heaven is like a net that was let down into the lake and caught all kinds of fish

"And he did not do many miracles there because of their lack of faith"

The kingdom of heaven is the sower, the seed, the treasure, the treasure hunter. The kingdom of heaven is the net that gathers all: the good and evil. This is one way to look at it, but I may be wrong. Starts with the sowing ends with the reaping.
