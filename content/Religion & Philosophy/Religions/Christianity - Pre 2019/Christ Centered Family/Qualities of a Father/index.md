FAMILY
- \~5 children each separated \~2 years appart. Though parents should be the role model within the household. There are things younger children can learn from the older and vice-versa. 10 year olds can learn from taking care of baby. toddlers can learn from teenagers. Note this is one whole family sharpening each other in things they fall short of. Even the parents can learn from their children. Makes sure all are in accordance to daily Bible reading. The father of the house is the leader. Where ever he goes the whole household follows. Make sure the father is a servant of God and the wife is in full support with him. Have nightly prayers with the whole family and just between the father and mother. So much time has to be dedicated that I'm starting to feel exhausted in thinking about it. In fact there is so much stress upon the father that he should prep himself years before meeting wife. Look up things a father should do.
- invite single friends into family's activities.
- as you get older nothing here on earth would be better than seeing your kids and grandkids grow in wisdom and stature, and in favor with God and man. What a blessing that would be for you. And it all starts with now.

Things a Father should Do:

Read Bible Daily
- read the Bible daily. Read it for the right reason and not for selfish ambition. Read to see the supremacy of God himself, realizing while reading it becomes a mirror reflecting the tainted image of God. Realizing everyone (that you know and do not) are also images of God himself. God created humankind in his image and we are to reflect that.
- I myself read the Bible and asked questions to not find an answer but to find an excuse to reject the Bible.
- do not fall for the tickling of your ears. The wind blows this way and that way. The chaffs follow wherever the wind goes. Do not be like the chaffs. The grass withers and the flower fades, but the Word of God stands forever. Put your self in a firm foundation where wind nor rain can wash away.
- it is interesting how Christians would take various sciencetific statements above the Truth of God. To them it is held higher within their mind, and God is placed second. They would question the Truth of God when science doesn't seem to agree and deem the Word a metaphor or false. It is okay to question the Word of God, but it is like walking along the fence. A place where you are on a tight rope and vulnerable to fall. Though this is not sin, it is not a great place stay at. Just like when Eve had her discourse with the servant. She said, "you must not eat fruit from the tree that is in the middle of the garden, and you must not touch it, or you will die" God never said that touching the fruit would cause you to die." Though it seems wise not to touch the fruit you are not to eat. Be aware where you mind swerves cause God has given you a choice and it tends to decay. Swim in the Word of God. Though Science is researched and backed by evidence. In no way are we saying Religion stops science altogether saying religion has all the answers. We don't have all the answers the Bible even says we don't have the answers. Being a christians is not about having all the answers. You don't need all the answers to all the questions in order to be a servant of God. (How do you know a mother caring for her child is actually loves her child? We don't need to know whats going on in the brain, we just know)
- spend much of time in God's Word by yourself. For "All people are like grass, and all their faithfulness is like the flowers of the field. The grass withers and the flowers fall, because the breath of the Lord blows on them. Surely the people are grass. The grass withers and the flowers fall, but the word of our God endures forever"

Find Various Mentors
- pastors, children pastors, those within you work domain senior to you, married couples, older single people, those who are in full time missionary. Anyone. Learn from the mistakes and strengths. Apply it to yourself.

Attend Small Group
- a place where you give and receive.
- build accountability

Learn to Write
- helpful for passing information exponentially

Learn how to Tell Stories
- helpful for teaching

Learn how to Talk with Anyone
- Intention - know your intention
- Approach - have good body language. Smile. "Hello, how are you?"
- Sparks - conversation sparks, non-verbal cues for engaging. Eye-brow raises. Find mutual likes till you find mutual spark.
- Last Impression is as Important as First - hey what you doing this weekend. Shift to Future Segment.
- no cell phone

Learn how to Speak
- helpful for teaching
- Listen as well

Learn how to Listen
- understand the various body language, and know how to respond and follow through
- helpful in responding well when speaking

Learn how to Teach
- a great way to give back what you learned
- teach for a couple of lessons in any subject area
- this is not a one way communication, apply lessons learned from Listening here.

Learn a Western and Eastern Language
- cause the grammar are spectacularly different.
- allows you to think differently
- opens doors

Learn a Musical Instrument
- preferably one you could give back
- provides a channel to be at peace

Learn how to choose what to learn
- most of the things I learned are from the internet. They are just way too much information and so many quotes to take.
- Know what to prune off, that is no one-size-fit-all answer here. But know your strengths and what you have in choosing what to learn.

All this takes time and it starts with now.
