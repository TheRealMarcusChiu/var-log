Significance of The Name of Jesus

- significance of the name Jesus - <http://www.auburn.edu/~allenkc/jname.html>
  - But in Bible study we are impressed with the idea that names are full of meaning:
    - Adam means "of the earth, or earthly" indicating man's origin who was formed from the dust of the ground and breathed into his nostrils the breath of life, and the man became a living being
    - Abel means feeder
    - Abraham - "means father of a great multitude" or "of many nations"
    - simon to peter - “Cephas” is a common noun meaning “stone,” or “rock.” Evidently, Jesus’ words were prophetic. He foresaw that Peter would become like a rock—a stable, sturdy, and reliable influence among Christ’s followers
  - As the wife takes the name of her husband, so the church takes the name of her Head. The two made one is the fact of importance
