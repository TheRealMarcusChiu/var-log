<strong>Philosophy of Religion</strong> deals with questions that involve religion and religious ideas from a philosophically neutral perspective (as opposed to theology which begins from religious convictions). Traditionally, religious questions were not seen as a separate field from philosophy proper, the idea of a separate field only arose in the 19th century.

### Issues Include

- existence of God
- relationship between <em>reason and faith</em>
- questions of <em>religious epistemology</em>
- relationship between religion and science
- how to interpret religious experiences
- questions about the possibility of an afterlife
- problem of religious language
- existence of spirits/souls
- responses to religious pluralism and diversity.

<strong>Origins of Religion</strong>

- an evolutionary adaptation for binding groups together and helping them to create communities with a shared morality
- arose from the environmental weather, explanations of environmental events
