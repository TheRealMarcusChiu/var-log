- <strong>inclusivism </strong>- asserts that while one set of beliefs is absolutely true, other sets of beliefs are at least partially true
  - one of several approaches to understanding the relationship between religions
- <strong>exclusivism</strong> - asserts that only one way is true and all others are in error
