- a <strong>phenomenon</strong> (Greek: φαινόμενον, <em>phainómenon</em>, from the verb φαίνειν, <em>phainein</em>, to show, shine, appear, to be manifest or manifest itself, plural <strong>phenomena</strong>) is an observable object, fact or event
- a <strong>noumenon</strong> (Greek: νούμενον) is a posited object or event that exists independently of human perception
