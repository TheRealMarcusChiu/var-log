- <strong>Positive Law</strong> (Latin: <em>ius positum</em>) human-made law that oblige or specify an action
- <strong>Natural Law</strong> (Latin: <em>ius naturale</em>, <em>lex naturalis</em>) as determined by nature, the <strong>law of nature</strong> is implied to be objective and universal; it exists independently of human understanding, and of the positive law
