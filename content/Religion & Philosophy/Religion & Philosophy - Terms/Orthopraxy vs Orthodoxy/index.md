# Orthopraxy
orthopraxy is correct conduct, both ethical and liturgical, as opposed to faith or grace etc. The word is a neoclassical compound—ὀρθοπραξία (orthopraxia) meaning 'correct practice'
# Orthodoxy
orthodoxy (contrasts orthopraxy) which emphasizes correct belief, and ritualism, the practice of rituals.
