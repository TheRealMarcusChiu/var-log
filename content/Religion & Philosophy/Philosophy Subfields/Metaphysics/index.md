###### Metaphysics
- examines the fundamental nature of reality
- this includes:
	- relationship between mind and matter
	- substance and attribute
	- possibility/potentiality and actuality
- the word "metaphysics" comes from 2 greek words, that literally mean "after or behind or among the \[study of\] the natural"
