###### Critical Realism

```excerpt
- is a philosophical approach associated with [Roy Bhaskar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roy_Bhaskar) (1944–2014), which combines a general [philosophy of science](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philosophy_of_science) ([transcendental realism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Transcendental_realism)) with a [philosophy of social science](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philosophy_of_social_science) ([critical naturalism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Critical_naturalism)) to dance an interface between the natural and social worlds
- is a heterogeneous series of positions:
	- [[Ontology - Objectivist vs Subjectivist|ontological realism]]
	- [[Epistemology - Objectivist vs Subjectivist vs Constructivist|epistemic relativism]]
	- judgmental rationality
	- cautious ethical naturalism
```

^excerpt
