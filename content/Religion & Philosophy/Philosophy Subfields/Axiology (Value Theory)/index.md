```excerpt
<strong>[[Axiology (Value Theory)]] </strong>- addresses topics such as goodness, beauty and justice
- <strong>[[Ethics (Moral Philosophy)]]</strong> - in terms of action. studies and considers what is: good and bad conduct, right and wrong values, and good and evil
- <strong>[[Aesthetics (Beauty／Beautiful)|Aesthetics]]</strong> - in terms of object. addresses the nature of art, beauty and taste, enjoyment, emotional values, perception and with the creation and appreciation of beauty
- <strong>Political Philosophy</strong> - study of government and the relationship of individuals (or families and clans) to communities including the state
- <strong>Philosophy of Law</strong><strong>(Jurisprudence)</strong> - explores the varying theories explaining the nature and interpretation of laws
- <strong>Philosophy of Education</strong> - analyzes the definition and content of education, as well as the goals and challenges of educators
- <strong>Feminist Philosophy</strong> - explores questions surrounding gender, sexuality and the body including the nature of feminism itself as a social and philosophical movement
- <strong>Philosophy of Sport</strong> - analyzes sports, games and other forms of play as sociological and uniquely human activities
```
