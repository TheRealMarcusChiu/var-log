# Knowledge Types
- <strong>[[Descriptive／Declarative／Propositional Knowledge|declarative/propositional/descriptive]]</strong> - assertions/propositional
- <strong>procedural</strong> - specifies what to do and when
- <strong>contextual</strong> - when to use the knowledge
- <strong>temporal</strong> - dealing with time
- <strong>inferential</strong> - not explicitly stated in a knowledge base, but could be derived/inferred by applying inferential/reasoning
- <strong>inherited</strong> - inferred via inheritance reasoning mechanism
- <strong>acquired</strong> - knowledge that is gathered either automatically by a K acquisition system or by a K engineer. acquired K must be merged with existing KB
