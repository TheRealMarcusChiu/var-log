###### Knowledge
- is the primary subject of [[Epistemology|epistemology]]
- on pre-[[Gettier Cases|Gettier]] epistemology, knowledge must satisfy three conditions (often called <em>justified-true-belief</em> JTB for short):
	- <strong>belief</strong> - existence of a belief (an acceptance that a proposition is true)
	- <strong>truth</strong> - the belief needs to be true
	- <strong>justification</strong> - the belief needs to be well supported. otherwise, the belief, even if it is true, may as well be a <em>lucky guess</em>. Thus the belief would be correct without being knowledge
