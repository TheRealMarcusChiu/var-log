###### <strong>Epistemology</strong>
- the study of knowledge, the:
	- processes and reasoning -
	- validity and justification -
	- scope and limitations -
- and to answer the question: "How do you know you know?"

### Terminology
- <strong>[[Knowledge|knowledge]]</strong> is a [[Propositions|proposition]] that must satisfy three conditions: justified-true-belief
- <strong>[[Propositions|proposition]]</strong> - a statement or assertion that expresses a judgment or opinion
- <strong>belief</strong> - an acceptance that a statement is true or that something exists
- [[Justification|justification]] - is the reason that someone (properly) holds a belief
- <strong>true/truth</strong>
	- first-order truth - being in accord with fact or reality
	- second-order truth - fidelity to an original or standard
- <strong>cognition</strong> - the mental action/processes of acquiring knowledge/understanding through sense-experience and/or reason
