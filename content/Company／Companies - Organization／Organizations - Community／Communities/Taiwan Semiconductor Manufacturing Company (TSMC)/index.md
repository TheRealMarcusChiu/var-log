###### Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (TSMC)
````excerpt
- is a Taiwanese multinational semiconductor contract manufacturing and design company
- it is the world's most valuable semiconductor company, the world's largest dedicated independent semiconductor foundry, and one of Taiwan's largest companies, with its headquarters and main operations located in the Hsinchu Science Park in Hsinchu
- it is majority owned by foreign investors
- TSMC has a 53.40% share of the global [[Semiconductor Companies|pure-play foundry]] market
````
^excerpt
