###### Cerebras Systems

```excerpt
- is an American artificial intelligence (AI) company founded in 2015 by Andrew Feldman, Gary Lauterbach, Michael James, Sean Lie, and Jean-Philippe Fricker
- specializes in building high-performance AI computing systems using its proprietary Wafer-Scale Engine (WSE) technology, which integrates compute, memory, and interconnect fabric on a single, large silicon wafer—making it significantly larger and more powerful than conventional chips
```
