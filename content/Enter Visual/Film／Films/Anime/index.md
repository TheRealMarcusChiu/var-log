# To Watch

- Deca-Dence = a post-apocalyptic sci-fi story with a really creative setup and a wild twist
- Eighty-Six = a futuristic mecha anime with a side helping of war drama and (later in the story where the anime hasn't reached yet) shojo romance
- Gurren Lagann = a post-apocalyptic mecha anime with constantly escalating hype that reaches a universal scale
- Magical Girl Lyrical Nanoha = a magical girl anime that slowly transforms into a sci-fi military anime over time
- Megalo Box = a cyberpunk boxing anime with awesome action and drama
- Outlaw Star = a space exploration adventure similar to Cowboy Bebop, but a bit more lighthearted and comedic
- Space Dandy = a space exploration adventure similar to Cowboy Bebop, but way more lighthearted and comedic
- Star Twinkle Precure = a magical girl anime that doubles as a space exploration adventure and has a lot of parodies of famous sci-fi works
- Symphogear = a magical girl anime that's heavily mecha-inspired (they look like Gundams turned into anime girls) and is also absolutely hype as fuck
