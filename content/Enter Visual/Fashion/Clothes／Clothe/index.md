# Designer Clothes - Why so Expensive?

one of the most obvious reasons designer clothes are so expensive is because you're paying for the brand name. The fashion industry is all about exclusivity and social status. People wear luxury brands for the quality, but also because they want others to know they are wearing name brand clothing
