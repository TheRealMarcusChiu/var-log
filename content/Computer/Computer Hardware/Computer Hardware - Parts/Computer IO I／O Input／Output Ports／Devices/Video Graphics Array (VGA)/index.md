###### Video Graphics Array (VGA)

```excerpt
- is a standard connector used for computer video output
- originating with the 1987 IBM PS/2 and its VGA graphics system, the 15-pin connector went on to become ubiquitous on PCs, as well as many monitors, projectors, and high-definition television sets
```
