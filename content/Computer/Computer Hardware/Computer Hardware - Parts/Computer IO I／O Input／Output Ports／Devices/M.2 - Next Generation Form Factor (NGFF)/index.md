###### M.2 - Next Generation Form Factor (NGFF)

```excerpt
- is a specification for internally mounted computer expansion cards and associated connectors
- M.2 replaces the Mini SATA (mSATA) standard and the Mini PCIe (mPCIe) standard
- connects to Motherboard via one of:
	- [[Peripheral Component Interconnect Express (PCIe - PCI-e) Specification／Interface|PCIe]] 3.0 ×4 lanes
	- PCIe 4.0 ×4 lanes
	- PCIe 5.0 ×4 lanes
	- [[Serial ATA (SATA) - Parallel ATA (PATA)|Serial ATA]] 3.0
	- [[Universal Serial Bus (USB) 2.0／3.0／3.2／4.0 Gen 1／2 (Specification／Interface)|USB]] 3.0
```

^excerpt
