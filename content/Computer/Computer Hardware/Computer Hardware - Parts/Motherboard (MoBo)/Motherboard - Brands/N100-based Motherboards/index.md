###### N100-based Motherboards
````excerpt
- are computer motherboards designed to be used with the [[Intel N100 Processor|Intel N100 processor]]
- are typically smaller in size, such as [[Motherboard - Sizes (ATX - ITX)|Mini-ITX or Micro-ATX]], and are often fanless, making them suitable for low-power applications like home media centers, NAS boxes, or simple desktop computers
- offer integrated graphics, typically Intel UHD Graphics with 24 EUs, and support a single DDR4 DIMM slot
````
