###### GIGABYTE MZ73-LM1 Motherboard
````excerpt
- Dual AMD EPYC™ 9005/9004 Series Processors
- 12-Channel DDR5 RDIMM, 24 x DIMMs
- 2 x 10Gb/s LAN ports via Broadcom<sup>®</sup> BCM57416
- 2 x SlimSAS 8i connectors with PCIe Gen4 x8 or SATA interface

- 4 x PCIe Gen4 x16 expansion slots
````
