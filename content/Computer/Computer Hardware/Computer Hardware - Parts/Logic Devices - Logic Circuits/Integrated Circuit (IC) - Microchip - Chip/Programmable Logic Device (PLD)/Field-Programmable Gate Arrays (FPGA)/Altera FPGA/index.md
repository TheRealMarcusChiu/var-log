###### Altera FPGA
- was a manufacturer of [[Programmable Logic Device (PLD)|programmable logic devices (PLDs)]]
- headquartered in San Jose, California
- it was founded in 1983 and acquired by [[Intel Corporation|Intel]] in 2015
