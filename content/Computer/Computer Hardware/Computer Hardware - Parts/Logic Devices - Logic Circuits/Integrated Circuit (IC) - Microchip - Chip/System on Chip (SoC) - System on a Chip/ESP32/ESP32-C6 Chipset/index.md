###### ESP32-C6 Chipset
````excerpt
- is a low-cost, low-power SoC (System on a Chip) developed by [[Espressif Systems]]
- it is a major milestone in the ESP32 family because it is Espressif’s first chipset to introduce Wi-Fi 6 connectivity, alongside a rich suite of IoT (Internet of Things) wireless protocols
- built on the open-source [[RISC-V]] architecture, it is designed for secure, power-efficient, and highly connected smart home and industrial IoT devices
````
