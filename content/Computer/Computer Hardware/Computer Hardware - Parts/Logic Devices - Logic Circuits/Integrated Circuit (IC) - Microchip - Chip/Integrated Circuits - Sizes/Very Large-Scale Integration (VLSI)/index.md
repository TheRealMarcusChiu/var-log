###### Very Large-Scale Integration (VLSI)
````excerpt
- refers to an [[Integrated Circuit (IC) - Microchip - Chip|IC]] or technology with many devices on one chip
- the question, of course, is how one defines "many." The term originated in the 1970s along with "SSI" (small-scale integration), "LSI" (large-scale), and several others, defined by the number of transistors or gates per IC
````
