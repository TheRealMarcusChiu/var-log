###### Bipolar Junction Transistors (BJT)

```excerpt
- is a type of [[Transistors|transistor]] that uses both electrons and electron holes as charge carriers
- in contrast, a unipolar transistor, such as a [[Field-Effect Transistor (FET)|field-effect transistor]], uses only one kind of charge carrier
```
