###### Complementary Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (CMOS)

- is a type of [[Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor (MOSFET - MOS-FET - MOS FET)|MOSFET]] fabrication process that uses complementary and symmetrical pairs of p-type and n-type MOSFETs for logic functions
