###### NMOS MOSFET Transistors

```excerpt
- when a HIGH voltage is applied to the gate, NMOS will conduct, while PMOS will not
- when a LOW voltage is applied in the gate, NMOS will not conduct and PMOS will conduct
```

^excerpt
