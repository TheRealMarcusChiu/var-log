###### Floating-Gate MOSFET (FGMOS) - Floating-Gate MOS Transistor - Floating-Gate transistor
- is a type of [[Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor (MOSFET - MOS-FET - MOS FET)|MOSFET]] where the gate is electrically isolated, thus storing non-volatile memory
- since the FG is surrounded by highly resistive material, the charge contained in it remains unchanged for long periods of time, nowadays typically longer than 10 years
- used as the digital storage element in EPROM, EEPROM, and [[Flash Memory|flash memory]] technologies
