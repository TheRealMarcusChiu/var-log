###### Field-Effect Transistor (FET)

```excerpt
- is a type of [[Transistors|transistor]] that uses an electric field to control the flow of current in a semiconductor
- are also known as <strong>unipolar transistors</strong> since they involve single-carrier-type operation
- the most widely used FET is the [[Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor (MOSFET - MOS-FET - MOS FET)|MOSFET]]
```

^excerpt
