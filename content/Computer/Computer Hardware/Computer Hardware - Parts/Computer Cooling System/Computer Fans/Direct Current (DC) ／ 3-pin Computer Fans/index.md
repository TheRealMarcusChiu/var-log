###### Direct Current (DC) Computer Fans - 3-pin Computer Fans

```excerpt
- are supplied with DC power from a regulated DC supply or motherboard header pins
- come with 3 pins:
	- supply pin (usually 12 V DC)
	- ground pin
	- signal pin
- speed of the fan is controlled by voltage from the supply pin
```
