###### Power Distribution Unit (PDU)
````excerpt
- is a device with multiple power outlets that provides electrical protection and distributes power to IT equipment within a rack
- can either be basic (also known as “dumb”) or intelligent, and there are multiple types of intelligent rack PDUs
````
