###### Power Supply Unit (PSU)

```excerpt
- converts the alternating current (AC) from a wall socket of mains electricity to a low-voltage direct current (DC) to operate the [[Motherboard (MoBo)|motherboard]], [[Processor - Processing Unit (PU) - Processing Element (PE)|processor]], and peripheral devices
```
