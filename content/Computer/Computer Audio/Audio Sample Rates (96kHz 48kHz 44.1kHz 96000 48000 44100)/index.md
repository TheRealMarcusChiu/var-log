###### Audio Sample Rates
````excerpt
- 44.1kHz remains a common and acceptable rate, particularly for music distribution on CDs and streaming
- 48kHz is often preferred for film and video production because it's the industry standard for those formats
- 96kHz sample rate is generally used in professional audio recording and production where capturing a wider range of audio frequencies and detail is desired
````
