###### Search Engine
````excerpt
- <font style="color: rgb(128,128,128);"><em>the ultimate search engine, ... would understand exactly what you need and give back exactly what you want</em></font>
- is both:
	- <strong>Index Server</strong> is assigned the task of 'crawling' your content and building up an Index of keywords and phrases
	- <strong>[[Search Servers|Search Server]]</strong> then uses this index to respond to user searches and suggest results
````
^excerpt

# Search Engine - Steps
1. analyze the meaning of words in the search query
2. matching them to the contents of the domain
3. understanding what content is most likely helpful and reliable

these 3 steps require some knowledge about [[Natural Language Processing (NLP) - Computational Linguistics]] and [[Linguistics]]
