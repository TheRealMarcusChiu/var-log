- <strong>[[Authentication (AuthN)|authentication]]</strong> is the process of verifying the identity of someone
- <strong>[[Authorization (AuthZ)|authorization]]</strong> is the process of verifying what someone is allowed to do
- <strong>accounting</strong> is the process of logging data on authentication and/or authorization events
- <strong>auditing</strong> is the process of inspecting logged data
- <strong>delegation</strong> is the process of delegating the authentication/authorization to another entity and trusting this entity's claims
- <strong>consent</strong> is the process of allowing an entity to act on your behalf
