[[Factory Method Pattern]] vs [[Template Method Pattern]]
- <font style="color: rgb(122,134,154);">Factory Method</font> is a specialization of <font style="color: rgb(122,134,154);">Template Method</font> used specifically for creating objects rather than containing business logic
- At the same time, a <font style="color: rgb(122,134,154);">Factory Method</font> may serve as a step in a large <font style="color: rgb(122,134,154);">Template Method</font>
