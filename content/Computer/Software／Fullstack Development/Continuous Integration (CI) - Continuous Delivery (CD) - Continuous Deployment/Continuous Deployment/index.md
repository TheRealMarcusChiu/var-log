<strong>[[Continuous Deployment]]</strong> - goes one step further than CD, where changes/<font style="color: rgb(128,128,0);">release-</font><font style="color: rgb(128,128,0);">artifacts</font> are automatically deployed to production, if they passed all stages of your production pipeline. If at any point a test failed the deployment to production is prevented. With Continuous Deployment there isn't a <em>Release Day</em> anymore
