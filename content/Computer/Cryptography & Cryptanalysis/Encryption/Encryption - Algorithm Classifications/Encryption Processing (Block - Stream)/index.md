[[Encryption - Algorithms|Encryption Algorithms]] are process plain-text in the following ways:

```excerpt
- <strong>block cipher</strong> is a method of encrypting text (to produce cipher-text) in which a cryptographic key and algorithm are applied to a block of data (for example, 64 contiguous bits) at once as a group rather than to one bit at a time
- <strong>stream cipher</strong> is a symmetric key cipher where plaintext digits are combined with a pseudorandom cipher digit stream (key-stream)
```

^excerpt
