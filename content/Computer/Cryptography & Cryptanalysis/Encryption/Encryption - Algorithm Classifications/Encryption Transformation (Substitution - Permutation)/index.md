an [[Encryption - Algorithms|Encryption Algorithm]] can utilize the following transformations:

```excerpt
- <strong>substitution cipher </strong>- substituting every occurrence of a symbol/group-of-bits with another symbol/group-of-bits
- <strong>permutation/transposition cipher </strong>- rearranging a sequence of symbols/bits to some other pseudorandom sequence
```

^excerpt

# Substitution Cipher Examples

- <strong>ROT13</strong> or <strong>ROT-13</strong> - is a simple letter substitution cipher that replaces a letter with the 13th letter after it, in the alphabet
