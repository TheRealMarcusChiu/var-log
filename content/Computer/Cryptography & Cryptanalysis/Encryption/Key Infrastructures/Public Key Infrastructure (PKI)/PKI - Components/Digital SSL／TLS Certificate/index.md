###### Digital SSL/TLS Certificate

```excerpt
- is a [[Certificate Signing Request (CSR)]] that has been <em>issued/signed</em> by a [[Certificate／Certification Authority (CA)|Certificate Authority]]
- certifies the ownership of a [[Asymmetric／Two／Public-Private Key Function|public key]] by the named subject of the digital certificate
```
