In [[Cryptography & Cryptanalysis|cryptography]], the <strong>International Data Encryption Algorithm</strong> (<strong>IDEA</strong>), originally called <strong>Improved Proposed Encryption Standard</strong> (<strong>IPES</strong>), is a [[Symmetric／Single／Secret Key Function|symmetric-key]]<em>block cipher</em> designed in 1991. The algorithm was intended as a replacement for the [[Data Encryption Standard (DES)|Data Encryption Standard (DES)]]
