###### Data Encryption Standard (DES)
````excerpt
- is a block cipher, [[Symmetric／Single／Secret Key Function|symmetric key algorithm]]. It’s being <font style="color: rgb(255,0,0);">phased out</font> in favor of faster methods like [[Advanced Encryption Standard (AES)|AES]]
````
^excerpt

# DES Types
- <strong>normal DES</strong> - bad
- <strong>Double DES</strong> - bad still, susceptible to Meet-in-the-Middle Attack
- <strong>Triple DES</strong> (3DES or TDES)
	- either:
		- 3 encryptions with 3 secret-keys
		- EDE mode - encrypt-decrypt-encrypt with 2 secret-keys
	- the problem with 3DES is that it’s too slow
