# Typeface - Classifications

2 most basic classifications:

- <strong>serif</strong> - contains any typeface with small “feet” on the end of letters
  - Times New Roman, Georgia, and Cambria
- <strong>sans serif</strong> - has no feet
  - Arial, Helvetica, Calibri, and Comic Sans
