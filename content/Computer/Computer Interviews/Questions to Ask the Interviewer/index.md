1. What are the most successful qualities of people that are most successful in this position?
2. If you were to give an offer and I was to accept. A year from now what will I have done specifically that you would consider this a hiring success?
3. If you were to give an offer and I was to accept. Within 1 week of starting this job, what will be the first thing that surprises me?
