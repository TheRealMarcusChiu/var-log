###### Internal Covariate Shift Problem
````excerpt
- is a phenomenon that occurs when the distribution of inputs to a neural network changes as the network's weights are updated during training
- this shift can slow down the training process because the hidden layers try to adapt to the new distribution
- this can be solved by applying [[ANN - Normalization Methods|normalization methods]]
````
^excerpt
