###### Gated Recurrent Unit (GRU)
````excerpt
- is a type of [[Gated Recurrent Neural Networks (Gated RNN)|gated RNN]]
- introduced in 2014 by Kyunghyun Cho et al
- the GRU is like an [[Long-Short Term Memory (LSTM)|LSTM]] with a forget gate, but has fewer parameters than LSTM, as it lacks an output gate
````
