###### Convolutional Kolmogorov-Arnold Networks (Convolutional KAN)

```excerpt
- an innovative alternative to the standard [[Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) - Translation／Translational／Shift Equivariant／Equivariance／Invariant／Invariance CNN|Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)]]
- integrated the [[Activation Functions (AF)|non-linear activation functions]] presented in [[Kolmogorov-Arnold Networks (KAN)|Kolmogorov-Arnold Networks (KANs)]] into [[ANN - Convolutional Layer|convolutions]] to build a new layer
- the performance of Convolutional KANs against traditional architectures across MNIST and Fashion-MNIST maintains a similar level of accuracy while using half the amount of parameters
```
