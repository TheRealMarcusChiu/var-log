###### Neocognitron

```excerpt
- is a hierarchical, multilayered [[Artificial Neural Networks (ANN)|artificial neural network]] proposed by Kunihiko Fukushima in 1979
- it has been used for Japanese handwritten character recognition and other pattern-recognition tasks
- served as the inspiration for [[Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) - Translation／Translational／Shift Equivariant／Equivariance／Invariant／Invariance CNN|convolutional neural networks]]
```

^excerpt
