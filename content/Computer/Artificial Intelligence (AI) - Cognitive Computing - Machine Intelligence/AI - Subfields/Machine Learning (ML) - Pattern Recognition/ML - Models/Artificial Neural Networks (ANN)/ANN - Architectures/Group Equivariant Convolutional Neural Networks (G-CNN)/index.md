###### Group Equivariant Convolutional Neural Networks (G-CNN)
- uses [[Algebraic Structures|group]]

# What it Offers
- create architectures with guarantees of invariance and/or equivariance
- equivariance allows for increased weight-sharing
- psychology of vision (recognition by components)
- efficient representation learning (leverage symmetries)
