###### Artificial Neural Networks (ANN)
````excerpt
- is a network of [[Perceptrons (Artificial Neurons)|perceptrons/neurons]]
- used in [[Deep Learning]]
- is a [[ML - Parametric vs Non-Parametric|parametric]], [[ML - Experience Type (Supervised／Unsupervised／Semi-Supervised／Self-Supervised／Multi-Instance／Reinforcement Learning)|supervised and/or unsupervised]] learning algorithm
- are very good [[Target Function／Distribution Predictor／Estimation／Estimator／Approximation／Approximators|function approximators]] (i.e. [[Universal Approximation Theory／Theorem|Universal Approximation Theorem]])
````
