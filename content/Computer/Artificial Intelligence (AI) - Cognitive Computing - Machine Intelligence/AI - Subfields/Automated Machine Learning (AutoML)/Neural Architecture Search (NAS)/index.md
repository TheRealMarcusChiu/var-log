###### Neural Architecture Search (NAS)
````excerpt
- is a technique in [[Automated Machine Learning (AutoML)|AutoML]] that automates the design of [[ANN - Architectures|artificial neural network architectures]] for specific tasks, often surpassing manually designed models in performance
- focuses on optimizing the topology of neural networks, including [[ANN - Layer Types|layer types]], connections, and operations, to achieve desired metrics such as accuracy, model size, or inference speed
````
