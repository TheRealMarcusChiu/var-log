###### Knowledge Graphs (KG)

- represents a collection of interlinked descriptions of entities – objects, events or concepts
- is a type of [[Knowledge Representation & Reasoning (KRR - KR² - KR&R) - Machine Reasoning (MR)|Knowledge Representation]]

# KG - Cons

- KG is not suited for tabular and time-series data
- KG expressiveness is limiting - putting stuff into triples is extremely limiting
