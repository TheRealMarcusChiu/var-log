###### Multiple View Geometry
````excerpt
- a basic problem in [[Computer Vision (CV)|computer vision]] is understanding the structure of a real-world scene given several images of it
- techniques for solving this problem are taken from <strong>projective geometry</strong> and <strong>photogrammetry</strong>
````
^excerpt
