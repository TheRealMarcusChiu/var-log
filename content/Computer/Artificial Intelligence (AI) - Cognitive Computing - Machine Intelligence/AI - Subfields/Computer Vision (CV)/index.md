<strong>Computer Vision (CV)</strong>

- is an interdisciplinary scientific field that deals with how computers can be made to gain a high-level understanding of digital images or videos
- from the perspective of engineering, it seeks to automate tasks that the human visual system can do
- a subfield of [[Machine Perception (MP)]]
