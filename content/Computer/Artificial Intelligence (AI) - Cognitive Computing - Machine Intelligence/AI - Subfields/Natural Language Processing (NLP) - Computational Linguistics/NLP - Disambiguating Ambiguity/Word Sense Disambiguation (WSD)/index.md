###### Word Sense Disambiguation (WSD)
- identifying the sense (i.e. meaning) of a word is used in a sentence (especially [[Homo／Hetero-nym／nymy／graph／phone - Synonym - Polyseme／Polysemy - Capitonym - Monosemy|homonyms/polysemes]] such as the word, bass which can be either a fish or an instrument)
- impacts other computer-related writing, such as discourse, improving relevance of search engines, [[Exophora - Endophora - Anaphora - Cataphora|anaphora]] [[Coreference／Co-Reference Extraction|coreference resolution]], [[Coherence (Relation) Extraction|coherence]], and inference
