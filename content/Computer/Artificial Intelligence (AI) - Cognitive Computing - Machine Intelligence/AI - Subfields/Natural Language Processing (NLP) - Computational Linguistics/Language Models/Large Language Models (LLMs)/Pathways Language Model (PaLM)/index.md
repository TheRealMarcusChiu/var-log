###### Pathways Language Model (PaLM)

```excerpt
- is a 540 billion-parameter dense decoder-only [[Transformer Neural Networks (TNN) - Transformers|transformer]]-based [[Large Language Models (LLMs)|large language model (LLM)]] developed by Google AI
- researchers also trained smaller versions of PaLM (with 8 and 62 billion parameters) to test the effects of model scale
- used in [Google Bard](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Google_Bard)
```

^excerpt
