###### LLM Interpretability
````excerpt
- is the field focused on understanding the internal workings of [[Large Language Models (LLMs)|large language models (LLMs)]] to reveal their reasoning processes, decision-making mechanisms, and the concepts they use to generate responses
- use techniques like [[Mechanistic Interpretability (Mech Interp - Mechinterp - MI) Research|mechanistic interpretability]] to map features to internal components, aiming to improve AI safety, fairness, alignment, and the reliability of LLMs by moving them from "black boxes" to more transparent systems
````
^excerpt
