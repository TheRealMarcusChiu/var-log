###### AutoGPT
````excerpt
- is an experimental open-source [[AI Agents／Assistants|AI agent]] framework that automates tasks by chaining together steps using a [[Large Language Models (LLMs)|large language model (LLM)]]
- unlike just asking an AI a single question, AutoGPT can plan, execute, and iterate on a series of actions autonomously toward achieving a goal you give it
````
