###### CrewAI
````excerpt
- is an [[AI Agents／Assistants|AI agent]] orchestration framework—a system for building, organizing, and running multiple autonomous AI agents that work together like a team to solve complex tasks
- rather than relying on a single AI “brain,” CrewAI lets you define multiple specialized agents (each with a role and goals) that communicate, delegate, and collaborate toward a shared objective
````
