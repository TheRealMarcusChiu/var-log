###### One-Hot Encoding

```excerpt
- a type of [[Word Embeddings／Embedding|word embedding]]
- each word is represented as a vector where:
	- its length equal to the size of the vocabulary/lexicon
	- has zeros everywhere except a 1 that corresponds to the word
```

^excerpt
