###### Machine Perception (MP)

- is the capability of a computer system to interpret data in a manner that is similar to the way humans use their senses to relate to the world around them
- a subfield of [[Artificial Intelligence (AI) - Cognitive Computing - Machine Intelligence|AI]]
- may use methods of [[Machine Learning (ML) - Pattern Recognition|Machine Learning / Pattern Recognition]]

# Subtypes

- [Machine vision](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Machine_perception#Machine_vision)
- [Machine hearing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Machine_perception#Machine_hearing)
- [Machine touch](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Machine_perception#Machine_touch)
- [Machine olfaction](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Machine_perception#Machine_olfaction)
- [Machine taste](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Machine_perception#Machine_taste)
