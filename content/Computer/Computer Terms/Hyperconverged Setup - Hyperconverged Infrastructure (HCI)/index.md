- <strong>Hyperconverged Setup</strong> - combines compute, networking, and storage into a single, manageable platform. This approach simplifies data center management, reduces complexity, and improves scalability.
- <strong>Hyperconverged Infrastructure (HCI)</strong> - can be deployed with a minimum of three nodes and expanded by adding more nodes as needed
