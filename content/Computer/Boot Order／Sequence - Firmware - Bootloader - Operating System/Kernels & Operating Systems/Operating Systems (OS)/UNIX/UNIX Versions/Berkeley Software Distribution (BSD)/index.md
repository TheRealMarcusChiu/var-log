###### Berkeley Software Distribution (BSD)
````excerpt
- is a [[UNIX|Unix]] [[Operating Systems (OS)|operating system]] derivative developed and distributed by the Computer Systems Research Group (CSRG) of the University of California, Berkeley, from 1977 to 1995
- today the term "BSD" is often used non-specifically to refer to any of the BSD descendants which together form a branch of the family of Unix-like operating systems
````
^excerpt
