###### RDNA (microarchitecture)
- is a [[Graphics Processing Unit (GPU) - Graphics Card|graphics processing unit]] (GPU) [[Computer Organization - Microarchitecture (µarch - uarch)|microarchitecture]] and accompanying [[Instruction Set Architecture (ISA)|instruction set architecture]] developed by [[Advanced Micro Devices (AMD)]]
- it is the successor to their Graphics Core Next (GCN) microarchitecture/instruction-set
- the first product lineup featuring RDNA was the Radeon RX 5000 series of video cards, launched on July 7, 2019
- it is manufactured and fabricated with TSMC's N7 FinFET graphics chips used in the Navi series of AMD Radeon graphics cards
