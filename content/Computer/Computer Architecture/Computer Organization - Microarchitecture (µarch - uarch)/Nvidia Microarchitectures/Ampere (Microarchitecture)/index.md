###### Ampere (Microarchitecture)
- is the codename for an [[Nvidia Microarchitectures|Nvidia microarchitecture]]
- successor to both the Volta and Turing microarchitectures
- officially announced on May 14, 2020
- fabrication processes:
	- TSMC 7 nm (Professional)
	- Samsung 8 nm (Consumer)
