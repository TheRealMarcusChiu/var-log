<strong>Spine and Leaf Architecture</strong> includes only two layers: spine and leaf.

- <strong>Spine Switch</strong> - the network core that interconnects all leaf switches. Spine switches are port dense and offer high throughput and speed and low latency to the leaf switches
- <strong>Leaf Switch</strong> - composed of access switches that connect directly to the spine
