###### Quad Small Form Factor Pluggable Double Density (QSFP-DD)
````excerpt
- the QSFP-DD transceiver is a new optical module comparable to the current [[QSFP - QSFP+|QSFP]] but adds an extra row of contacts for an eight-lane electrical interface
- all QSFP-DD-based transceivers from 40G to 200G is backward compatible with [[QSFP - QSFP+|QSFP+]]
- in a single rack unit (RU), QSFP-DD can support 36 400GbE ports, with a total bandwidth of nearly 14Tb/s
````
