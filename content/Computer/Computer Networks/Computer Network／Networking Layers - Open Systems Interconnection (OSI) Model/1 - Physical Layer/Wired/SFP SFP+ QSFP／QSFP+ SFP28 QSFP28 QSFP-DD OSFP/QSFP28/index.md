###### QSFP28

```excerpt
- is a new form factor defined in SFF Committee specifications SFF-8636 and SFF-8665
- with the capability of four data lanes (quad) in the single module, QSFP28 provides 100Gbps or 128Gbps aggregated data speed
- it shares the same principle as [[QSFP - QSFP+|QSFP+]]; the critical difference is the data rate
- generally, it is intended for 100 Gigabit [[802.3 - Ethernet|Ethernet]], EDR [[InfiniBand (IB)|InfiniBand]], or 32G [[Fibre Channel (FC)|Fibre Channel]] networks
- using a QSA adapter, you may also convert the 100G QSFP28 port into the SFP28 port
- in the 100G optics market, QSFP28 transceivers are more popular than CFP, CFP2, and CFP4 form factors
```

^excerpt
