###### QSFP112

```excerpt
- is an improved version of [[QSFP28]] that supports a much higher speed of 112Gbps per lane for a 400G system
- it was introduced in 2021 by QSFP-DD MSA ([www.qsfp-dd.com](http://www.qsfp-dd.com))
- like the [[QSFP - QSFP+|QSFP+]]/[[QSFP28]] module and connectors, QSFP112 has identical dimensions
- it targets legacy QSFP+/QSFP28 users looking to upgrade smoothly and quickly to 400G networks
```

^excerpt
