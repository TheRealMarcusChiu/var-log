###### Passive Optical Network (PON)
````excerpt
- is a fiber-optic telecommunications network that uses only unpowered devices to carry signals, as opposed to electronic equipment
- in practice, PONs are typically used for the last mile between Internet service providers (ISP) and their customers
````
