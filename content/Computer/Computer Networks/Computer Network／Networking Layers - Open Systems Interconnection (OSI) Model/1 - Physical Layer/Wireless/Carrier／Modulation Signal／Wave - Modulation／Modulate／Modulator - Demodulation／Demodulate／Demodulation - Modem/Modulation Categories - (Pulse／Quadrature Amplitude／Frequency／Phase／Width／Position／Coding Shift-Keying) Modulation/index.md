- <strong>Analog Modulation</strong> - impresses an analog modulation signal onto the carrier signal, examples are:
  - [amplitude modulation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amplitude_modulation) (AM) in which the amplitude of the carrier wave is varied by the modulation signal
  - [frequency modulation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frequency_modulation) (FM) in which the frequency of the carrier wave is varied by the modulation signal
- <strong>Digital Modulation</strong> - impresses a [digital signal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Digital_signal) onto the carrier signal
