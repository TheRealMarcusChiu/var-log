Peer-to-peer computing or networking is a [[Distributed - Algorithms／Computing／Programming／Systems|distributed]] application architecture that partitions tasks or workloads between peers. Peers are equally privileged, equipotent participants in the application. They are said to form a peer-to-peer network of nodes
