###### Quantum Information Theory
````excerpt
- is the study of how information processing tasks—such as storage, transmission, and manipulation—can be accomplished using quantum mechanical systems, which exhibit unique properties not found in classical systems
- extends classical [[Information Theory - Mathematical Theory of Communication - Telecommunications|information theory]]
````
