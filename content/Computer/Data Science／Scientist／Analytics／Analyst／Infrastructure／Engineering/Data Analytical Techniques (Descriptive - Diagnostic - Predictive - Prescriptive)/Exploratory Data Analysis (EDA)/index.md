###### Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- is associated with [[Descriptive Statistics]] with its approach in analyzingdata sets to summarize their main characteristics
- sometimes also include [[Inferential Statistics]] with its approach in seeing what the data can tell us beyond the formal modeling or [[Inferential Statistical Hypothesis Testing|hypothesis testing task]]
