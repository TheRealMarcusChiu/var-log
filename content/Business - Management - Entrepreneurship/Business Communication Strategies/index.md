# First Figure Out the Why You Are Creating a Strategy?

possible whys (not exhuastive):
- Is the communication strategy designed to create awareness of our brand/company to our potential <strong>customers</strong>?
- Is the communication strategy designed to create awareness of our brand/company to our potential <strong>investors</strong>?
- Is the communication strategy designed to create awareness of our brand/company to our potential <strong>acquirers</strong>?

# Second Figure Out the How You Are Creating a Strategy?

There are 4 steps:
1. Who is your audience? Understand your audience.
	1. is it the customers, investors, or acquirers
2. what's the message for that specific audience?
	1. The message should answer these questions:
		1. Why should the audience care?
		2. What's the offer?
		3. What's the call to action?
	2. The message should be memorable. It should be sticky.
		1. a classic analogy is, imagine you are being told you should pay for cold dead fish wrapped in seaweed. you might not be too hungry. But when we call it sushi we line up and pay lots of money.
3. what is the media and channels that you want the message to be read heard or seen on?
4. who is the messenger, that you want to carry the message?
