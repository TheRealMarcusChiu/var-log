###### Hyperbolic Geometry
````excerpt
- two parallel lines are taken to converge in one direction and diverge in the other.
- in [[Euclidean Geometry|Euclidean geometry]], the sum of the angles in a triangle is equal to two right angles; in hyperbolic geometry, the sum is less than two right angles
````
