a<strong> Simplicial Complex</strong> 𝐾 is a set of [[Simplex - Simplices|simplices]] that satisfies the following conditions:

- every face of a simplex from 𝐾 is also in 𝐾
- the non-empty intersection of any two simplices 𝜎<sub>1</sub>,𝜎<sub>2</sub>∊𝐾 is a face of both 𝜎<sub>1</sub> and 𝜎<sub>2</sub>

see also the definition of an [abstract simplicial complex](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abstract_simplicial_complex), which loosely speaking is a simplicial complex without an associated geometry
