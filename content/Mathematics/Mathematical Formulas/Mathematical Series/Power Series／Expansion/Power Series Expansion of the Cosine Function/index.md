The [[Trigonometric／Circular／Angle／Goniometric Functions (sine - cosine - tangent - cosecant - secant - cotangent)|cosine function]] 𝑐𝑜𝑠(𝑥) can be expanded into a [[Power Series／Expansion|power series]]:

- $cos(x) := \frac{x^0}{0!} - \frac{x^2}{2!} + \frac{x^4}{4!} - \frac{x^6}{6!} + \frac{x^8}{8!} ...$
