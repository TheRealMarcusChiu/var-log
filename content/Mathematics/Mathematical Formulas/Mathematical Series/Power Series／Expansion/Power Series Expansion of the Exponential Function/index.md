The [[Exponential Function|exponential function]] 𝑒𝑥𝑝(𝑥) can be expanded into a [[Power Series／Expansion|power series]]:

- $exp(x) := \sum_{k=0}^∞\frac{x^k}{k!} = \frac{x^0}{0!} + \frac{x^1}{1!} + \frac{x^2}{2!} + \frac{x^3}{3!} + ...$

See: [[Taylor Series Expansion of Exponential Function]]

# Other
