###### Chernoff Bound

```excerpt
- In [[Probability Theory|probability theory]], a <strong>Chernoff bound</strong> is an exponentially decreasing upper bound on the tail of a random variable based on its [[Moment-Generating Functions (MGF)|moment-generating function]]
```

^excerpt

# Statement

- $𝐏(X≥a) ≤ e^{-at} M_X(t)$
