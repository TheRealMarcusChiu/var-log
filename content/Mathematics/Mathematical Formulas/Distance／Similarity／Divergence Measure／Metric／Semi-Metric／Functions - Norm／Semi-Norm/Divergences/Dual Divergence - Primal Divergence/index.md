The <strong>dual divergence</strong> 𝐷\* is defined as:
- 𝐷\*(𝑝,𝑞) = 𝐷(𝑞,𝑝)

When we wish to contrast 𝐷 against 𝐷\*, we refer to 𝐷 as <strong>primal divergence</strong>
