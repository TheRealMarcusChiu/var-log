When you are [[Distance Measures／Metrics／Semi-Metrics／Functions|measuring by DISTANCE]], the most closely related points will have the LOWEST distance

When you are [[Similarity Measures／Metrics／Semi-Metrics／Functions|measuring by SIMILARITY]], the most closely related points will have the HIGHEST similarity
