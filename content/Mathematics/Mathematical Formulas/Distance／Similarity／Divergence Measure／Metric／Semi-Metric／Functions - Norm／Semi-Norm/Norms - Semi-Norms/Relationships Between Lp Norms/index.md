The [[L2／Euclidean Norm|L2-norm (||·||2)]] of any vector 𝑥 is bounded by its [[L1／Absolute Norm|L1-norm (||·||1)]]:

- ||𝑥||<sub>2</sub> ≤ ||𝑥||<sub>1</sub>

This fact generalizes to [[Lp／Minkowski Norm (p-norm)|L𝑝-norms]] in that the [[Lp／Minkowski Norm (p-norm)|L𝑝-norm (||·||𝑝)]] of any given vector 𝑥 does not grow with 𝑝:

- ||𝑥||<sub>𝑝+𝑎</sub> ≤ ||𝑥||<sub>𝑝</sub>

for any vector 𝑥 and real numbers 𝑝≥1 and 𝑎≥0. (In fact, this remains true for 0<𝑝<1 and 𝑎≥0)
