###### Rational Trigonometry

- follows an approach built on the methods of [[Linear Algebra|linear algebra]] to the topics of elementary (high school level) [[Geometry|geometry]]
- where:
  - distance is replaced with its squared value (quadrance)
  - angle is replaced with the squared value of the usual sine ratio (spread) associated with either angle between two lines
