- <strong>[[Equivariant／Equivariance|equivariance]]</strong> - how do brains compute and REPRESENT TRANSFORMATIONS (i.e. variations in pose, motion, lighting, etc)
- <strong>[[Invariant／Invariance|invariance]]</strong>   - how do brains compute and REPRESENT OBJECTS independent from TRANSFORMATIONS
