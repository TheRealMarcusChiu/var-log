# Important Special Cases Include

- <em>exponential map</em> ([[Riemannian Geometry|Riemannian geometry]]) for a [[Manifolds (n-Dimensional Manifold (n-Manifold)|manifold]] with a [[Riemannian Metric - Riemannian Metric Tensor|Riemannian metric]]
- <em>exponential map</em> ([[Lie Theory|Lie theory]]) from a Lie algebra to a [[Lie Groups|Lie group]]
- More generally, in a [[Manifolds (n-Dimensional Manifold (n-Manifold)|manifold]] with an affine connection, 𝑋 → 𝛾<sub>𝑋</sub>(1), where 𝛾<sub>𝑋</sub> is a geodesic with initial velocity 𝑋, is sometimes also called the <em>exponential map</em>. The above two are special cases of this with respect to appropriate affine connections
