###### Finite Difference Method (FDM)

- are a class of numerical techniques for solving [[Differential Equations (DE) - Ordinary Differential Equations (ODEs) - Partial Differential Equations (PDEs)|differential equations]] by approximating derivatives with [finite differences](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Finite_difference_approximation)
