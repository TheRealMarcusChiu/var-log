###### Rigid Geometric Algebra (RGA)
````excerpt
- is a type of [[Geometric Algebra (GA) - Clifford Algebra|geometric algebra]]
- adds one dimension to ordinary space and incorporates representations for Euclidean points, lines, and planes as well as operations for performing rotations, reflections, and translations
- it completely subsumes conventional models that include homogeneous coordinates, Plücker coordinates, quaternions, and screw theory (which makes use of dual quaternions)
````
^excerpt
