###### Blades - k-Blades
````excerpt
- <strong>blades</strong> are the combined [[Geometric Product|geometric products]] between [[Scalars|scalars]] and [[Vectors|vectors]] of an [[Orthonormal Basis Vectors|orthonormal basis]] in the associated [[Vector Spaces - Linear Spaces|vector space]]
- a blade's <strong>grade</strong> is the number of basis vectors that are multiplied together (after it is reduced)
	- the geometric product of \>𝑛 vectors can always be reduced to a geometric product with ≤𝑛 vectors, where 𝑛 is the dimension of the associated vector space
- a blade of grade k is called a <strong>k-blade</strong>
- permutations of the same product end up being scaled versions of each other, so it is possible to define a set of blades as a basis for the vector space of multivectors by choosing one of each permutation set
	- the standard basis of ℝ<sup>3</sup> {𝑒<sub>1</sub>, 𝑒<sub>2</sub>, 𝑒<sub>3</sub>} leads to this standard basis of blades for 𝔾<sup>3</sup> {1, 𝑒<sub>1</sub>, 𝑒<sub>2</sub>, 𝑒<sub>3</sub>, 𝑒<sub>1</sub>𝑒<sub>2</sub>, 𝑒<sub>1</sub>𝑒<sub>3</sub>, 𝑒<sub>2</sub>𝑒<sub>3</sub>, 𝑒<sub>1</sub>𝑒<sub>2</sub>𝑒<sub>3</sub>}
- a k[[k-vectors (Multivector of Grade k)|-vector]] is a linear combination of <em>k-blades</em>
- etc
````
