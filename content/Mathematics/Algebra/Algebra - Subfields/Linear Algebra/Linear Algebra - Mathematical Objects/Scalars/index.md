###### Scalars
````excerpt
- are often [[Real Numbers System|real numbers]], but can be [[Complex Numbers System|complex numbers]] or, more generally, elements of any [[Algebraic Structures|field]]
- the word scalar comes from Latin and has a sense of <em>changing the scale</em> of something
- is a type of [[k-vectors (Multivector of Grade k)|k-vector]] where k=0
- encodes magnitude ONLY
````
^excerpt
