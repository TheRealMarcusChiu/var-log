###### QR decomposition - QR factorization - QU factorization

- is a type of [[Matrix - Decompositions／Factorizations|matrix decomposition]] such that 𝐴 = 𝑄𝑅

where:

- 𝑄 - [[Orthogonal／Orthonormal Matrix|orthogonal matrix]]
- 𝑅 - upper triangular matrix

### QR Decomposition - Computation

There are several methods computing the 𝑄𝑅 decomposition each with their advantages and disadvantages:

- [Gram–Schmidt process](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gram%E2%80%93Schmidt_process)
- [Householder transformations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Householder_transformation)
- [Givens rotations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Givens_rotation)
