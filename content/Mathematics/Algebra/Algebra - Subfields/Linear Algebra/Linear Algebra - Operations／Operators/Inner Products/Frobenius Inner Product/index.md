###### Frobenius Inner Product (⟨·,·⟩<sub>𝐹</sub>)
````excerpt
- is a type of [[Inner Products|inner product (⟨·,·⟩)]] that takes [[Matrix／Matrices|matrices]] as input
- is defined as
	- $⟨A,B⟩_F = \sum_{i,j}\overline{A_{ij}}B_{ij} = Tr(\overline{A^T}B) = Tr(A^{\dagger}B)$
	- where the overline denotes the [[Complex Conjugate／Conjugation|complex conjugate]], and † denotes the [[Transpose Matrix - Complex-Conjugate／Hermitian／Adjoint／Transjugate／Bedaggered Transpose Matrix|Hermitian conjugate]]
- ⟨·,·⟩<sub>𝐹</sub>induces a [[LF／Frobenius／Hilbert-Schmidt Norm|Frobenius Norm (||·||𝐹)]] defined as: [[LF／Frobenius／Hilbert-Schmidt Norm|||·||𝐹]] = √(⟨·,·⟩<sub>𝐹</sub>)
````
^excerpt
