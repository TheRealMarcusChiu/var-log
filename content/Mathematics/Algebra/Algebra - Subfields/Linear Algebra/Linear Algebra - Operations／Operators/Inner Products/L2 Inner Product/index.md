###### L<sup>2</sup> Inner Product (⟨·,·⟩<sub>L2</sub>)

```excerpt
- is a type of [[Inner Products|inner product (⟨·,·⟩)]] that takes two real functions 𝑓 and 𝑔 on a [[Measure Spaces|measure space (𝑋,𝐴,𝜇)]] with respect to the [[Measure／Measures|measure (𝜇)]]
	- $⟨f,g⟩_{L^2} = \int_X f \; g \; d𝜇$
```

^excerpt
