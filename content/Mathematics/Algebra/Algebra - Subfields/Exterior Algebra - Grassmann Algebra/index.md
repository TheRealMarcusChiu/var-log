###### Exterior Algebra - Grassmann Algebra
````excerpt
- named after Hermann Grassmann
- is an [[Algebra|algebra]] that uses the [[Wedge Product - Exterior Product|exterior/wedge product]] as its multiplication operation
- is the part of [[Geometric Algebra (GA) - Clifford Algebra|geometric algebra]] that just uses the [[Wedge Product - Exterior Product|exterior/wedge product]] (which means a lot of geometric manipulation can be done without involving the [[Geometric Product|geometric product]])
````
^excerpt
