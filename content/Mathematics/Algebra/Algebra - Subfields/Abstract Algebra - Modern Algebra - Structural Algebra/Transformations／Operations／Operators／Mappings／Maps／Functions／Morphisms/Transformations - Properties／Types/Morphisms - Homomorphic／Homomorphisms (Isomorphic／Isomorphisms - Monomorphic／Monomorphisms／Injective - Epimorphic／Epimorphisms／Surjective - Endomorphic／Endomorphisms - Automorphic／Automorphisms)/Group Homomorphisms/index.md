###### Group Homomorphisms

```excerpt
- is any function 𝑓 : 𝐺 → 𝐺 that acts on elements of a [[Groups (Algebraic Structure) - Group Theory|group (𝐺,·)]] such that:
	- 𝑓(𝑎·𝑏) = 𝑓(𝑎) · 𝑓(𝑏)
```

^excerpt
