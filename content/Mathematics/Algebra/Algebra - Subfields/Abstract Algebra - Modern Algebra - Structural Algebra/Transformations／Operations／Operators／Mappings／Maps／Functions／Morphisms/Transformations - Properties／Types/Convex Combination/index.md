given 𝑛 points 𝑥<sub>1</sub>, 𝑥<sub>2</sub>, …, 𝑥<sub>𝑛</sub> ∈ 𝑋 we call any point 𝑦 a <strong>convex</strong> <strong>combination</strong> of those 𝑛 points iff:
- 𝑦 = 𝛴<sub>1≤</sub><sub>𝑖≤𝑛</sub>\[𝜆<sub>𝑖</sub>𝑥<sub>𝑖</sub>\]

where:
- 𝜆<sub>1</sub>, 𝜆<sub>2</sub>, …, 𝜆<sub>𝑛</sub>≥ 0
- 𝛴<sub>1≤</sub><sub>𝑖≤𝑛</sub>\[𝜆<sub>𝑖</sub>\] = 1
