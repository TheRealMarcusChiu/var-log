###### Operator Norm (||·||<sub>𝑜𝑝</sub>)

```excerpt
- is a [[Norms - Semi-Norms|norm (||·||)]] for [[Bounded／Unbounded Linear Operator (Transformations／Operations／Operators／Mappings／Maps／Functions)|bounded linear operators (𝐿)]] between two given [[Normed Vector Spaces - Normed Spaces|normed vector spaces (𝑉,𝐹,||·||)]]
- informally, the <em>operator norm </em><em>‖𝐿‖<sub>𝑜𝑝</sub></em> of a [[Linear (Transformations／Operations／Operators／Mappings／Maps／Functions／Morphisms) - Vector Space Homomorphism|linear operator]] 𝐿: 𝑋→𝑌 is the maximum factor by which it "lengthens" vectors
- the <em>operator norm</em> of 𝐿 is equal to the largest [[Singular Values (s-numbers)|singular value]] of 𝐿
```

^excerpt
