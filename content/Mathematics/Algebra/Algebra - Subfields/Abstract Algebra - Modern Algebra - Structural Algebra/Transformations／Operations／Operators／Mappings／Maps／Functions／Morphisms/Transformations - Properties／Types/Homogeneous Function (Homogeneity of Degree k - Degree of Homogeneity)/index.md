###### Homogeneous Function (Homogeneity of Degree k - Degree of Homogeneity)
````excerpt
- is a [[Functions (Domain - Codomain - Preimage - Image - Range)|function]] of 1 or more variables such that, if all its arguments are multiplied by a scalar, then its value is multiplied by some power of this scalar, called degree of homogeneity, or simply the degree
- i.e. if 𝑘 is an integer, a function 𝑓 of 𝑛 inputs is homogeneous of degree 𝑘 if:
	- 𝑓(𝑠𝑥<sub>1</sub>, ..., 𝑠𝑥<sub>𝑛</sub>) = 𝑠<sup>𝑘</sup>𝑓(𝑥<sub>1</sub>, ..., 𝑥<sub>𝑛</sub>) for all 𝑥<sub>1</sub>, ..., 𝑥<sub>𝑛</sub> and 𝑠 ≠ 0
````
^excerpt
