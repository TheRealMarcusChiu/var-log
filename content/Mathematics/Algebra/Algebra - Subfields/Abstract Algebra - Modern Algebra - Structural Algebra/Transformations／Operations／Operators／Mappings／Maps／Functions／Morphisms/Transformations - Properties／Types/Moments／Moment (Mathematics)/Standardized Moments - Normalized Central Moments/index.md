###### Standardized Moments - Normalized Central Moments

```excerpt
- is a type of [[Moments／Moment (Mathematics)|moment]]
- are [dimensionless quantities](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dimensionless_number), which represent the distribution independently of any linear change of scale
```

^excerpt

# Definition

The 𝑛<sup>th</sup> standardized moment is the 𝑛<sup>th</sup> central moment divided by 𝜎<sup>𝑛</sup>:

- $\frac{𝜇_n}{𝜎^n} = \frac{E[(X-𝜇)^n]}{𝜎^n} = \frac{E[(X-𝜇)^n]}{E[(X-𝜇)^2]^{n/2}}$
