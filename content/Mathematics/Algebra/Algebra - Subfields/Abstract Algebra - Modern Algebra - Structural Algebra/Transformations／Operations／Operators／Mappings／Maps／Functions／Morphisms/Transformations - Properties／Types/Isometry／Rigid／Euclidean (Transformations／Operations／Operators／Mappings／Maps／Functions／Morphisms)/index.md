###### Isometry/Rigid/Euclidean (Transformations/Operations/Operators/Mappings/Maps/Functions/Morphisms)
````excerpt
- is a geometric [[Transformations／Operations／Operators／Mappings／Maps／Functions／Morphisms|transformation]] between [[Metric Spaces|metric spaces]] that preserves [[L2／Euclidean Distance Metric|Euclidean distance]]
- usually assumed to be [[Function - Injective／Injection／One-to-one - Surjective／Surjection／Unto／Onto - Bijective／Bijection／One-to-one Correspondence|bijective]]
- examples include: [[Rotational Matrix|rotations]], translations, reflections, and their [[Permutations - Combinations - Total Combination|permutations]]
- a <em>rigid transformation</em> that doesn't include reflections is called a [[Proper Rigid (Transformations／Operations／Operators／Mappings／Maps／Functions／Morphisms) - Rototranslation|proper rigid transformation]]
````
