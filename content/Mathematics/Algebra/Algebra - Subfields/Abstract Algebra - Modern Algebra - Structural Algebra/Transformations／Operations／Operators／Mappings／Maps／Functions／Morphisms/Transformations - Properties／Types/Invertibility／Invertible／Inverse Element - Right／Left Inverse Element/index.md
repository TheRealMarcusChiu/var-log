# Inverse - Definition

Given:

- [[Transformations／Operations／Operators／Mappings／Maps／Functions／Morphisms|operation (∗)]] over [[Set Theory|set (𝑆)]]
- [[Identity／Neutral Element - Right／Left Identity Element|identity element (𝑒)]] of 𝑆
- 𝑥 and 𝑦 are some elements of 𝑆

if:

- 𝑥 ∗ 𝑦 = 𝑒

then:

- 𝑥 is the <strong><em>left inverse</em></strong> of 𝑦
- 𝑦 is the <strong><em>right inverse</em></strong> of 𝑥
