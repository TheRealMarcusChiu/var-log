- <strong>interior</strong> - the <em>interior</em> of a subset 𝑆 of space 𝑋 is the union of all subsets of 𝑆 that are [[Mathematical Space (Open Sets - Closed Sets - Clopen Sets)|open]] in 𝑋
- <strong>exterior</strong> - the <em>exterior</em> of a subset 𝑆 of space 𝑋 is the complement of the [[Mathematical Space (Closures - Spans - Generated Sets)|closure]] of 𝑆
