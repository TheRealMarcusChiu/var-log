###### Basis (𝐵)
````excerpt
- a <em>basis</em> 𝐵 is a subset of elements of a [[Mathematical Spaces|mathematical space (𝑋)]] such that every element 𝑥 in 𝑋 can be represented uniquely as a combination of elements in 𝐵
- most often referred to as [[Basis Vectors]]
````
