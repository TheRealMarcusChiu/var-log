###### [[Basis Functions]] vs [[Basis Vectors]]

- in [[Finite-Dimensional Vector Spaces|finite-dimensional vector space]], we only need finite vectors to construct a complete basis set
- in [[Function Spaces|function space]], we may need infinite basis functions to construct a complete basis set
