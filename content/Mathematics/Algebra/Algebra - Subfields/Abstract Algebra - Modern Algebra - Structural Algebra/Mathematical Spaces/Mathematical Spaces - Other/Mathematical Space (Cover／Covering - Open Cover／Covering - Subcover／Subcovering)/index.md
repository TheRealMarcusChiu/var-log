###### Cover/Covering (𝐶)
- a <strong>cover</strong> of a set/space 𝑋 is a family of subsets of 𝑋 whose union is all of 𝑋
- a <strong>cover</strong> of a subset 𝑌 of 𝑋 is a family of subsets of 𝑋 whose union contains all of 𝑌

###### Open Cover/Covering
- an <strong>open cover</strong> is a <em>cover</em> whose elements are all [[Mathematical Space (Open Sets - Closed Sets - Clopen Sets)|open sets]]

###### Subcover/Subcovering
- let 𝐶 be a <em>cover</em> of a space 𝑋; a <strong>subcover</strong> of 𝐶 is a subset of 𝐶 that still covers 𝑋
