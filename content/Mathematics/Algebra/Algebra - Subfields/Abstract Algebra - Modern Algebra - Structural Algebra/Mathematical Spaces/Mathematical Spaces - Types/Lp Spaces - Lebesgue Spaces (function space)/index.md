###### L<sup>p</sup> Spaces - Lebesgue Spaces (𝐿<sup>𝑝</sup>)

```excerpt
- is a type of [[Mathematical Spaces|mathematical space]]
- is a type of functional [[Quotient Spaces (linear algebra)|quotient space]]
- are [[Function Spaces|function spaces]] defined using a natural generalization of the [[Lp／Minkowski Norm (p-norm)|p-norm]] for [[Finite-Dimensional Vector Spaces|finite-dimensional vector spaces]]
- all 𝐿<sup>𝑝</sup> are normed complete vector spaces
- form an important class of [[Banach Spaces|Banach spaces]] in [[Functional Analysis|functional analysis]] and topological vector spaces
- important in the [[Mathematical Analysis|mathematical analysis]] of [[Measure Spaces|measure spaces]] and [[Probability Spaces (Sample Space - Event Space - Probability Measure)|probability spaces]]
```
