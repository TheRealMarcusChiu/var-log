# Linear Subspace - Definition

Given:
- a [[Vector Spaces - Linear Spaces|vector space (𝑉,𝐹)]] over a [[Fields (Algebraic Structure) - Field Theory|field (𝐹)]]
- a subset 𝑊 of 𝑉

𝑊 is a <em>linear subspace</em> of 𝑉 if, under the operations of 𝑉, 𝑊 is a vector space over 𝐹.

Equivalently, a nonempty subset 𝑊 is a subspace of 𝑉 if:
- (𝛼𝑤<sub>1</sub> + 𝛽𝑤<sub>2</sub>) ∊ 𝑊 for all 𝑤<sub>1</sub>,𝑤<sub>2</sub>∊𝑊 and 𝛼,𝛽∊𝐹
