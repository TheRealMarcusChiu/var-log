###### Inner Product Spaces (𝑉,𝐹,⟨·,·⟩)
````excerpt
- is a type of [[Mathematical Spaces|mathematical space]]
- an [[Inner Products|inner product (⟨·,·⟩)]] induces a [[Norms - Semi-Norms|norm (||·||⟨·,·⟩)]] defined as: ||·||<sub>⟨·,·⟩</sub> = √⟨·,·⟩
	- thus all <em>inner product spaces (𝑉,𝐹,⟨·,·⟩)</em> are also [[Normed Vector Spaces - Normed Spaces|normed vector spaces (𝑉,𝐹,||·||⟨·,·⟩)]]
````
^excerpt

# Inner Product Spaces - Examples
- Every <em>finite inner product space</em> is a [[Hilbert Spaces|Hilbert space (𝓗)]]
