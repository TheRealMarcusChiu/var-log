###### Normed Complete Vector Spaces - Complete Normed Linear Spaces

```excerpt
- is a type of [[Mathematical Spaces|mathematical space]]
- is a real or complex [[Vector Spaces - Linear Spaces|vector space]] in which:
	- each [[Vectors|vector]] has a non-negative length, norm (i.e. [[Normed Vector Spaces - Normed Spaces|normed vector space]])
	- every [[Mathematical Space (Cauchy Sequences)|Cauchy sequence]] converges to a point in the space (i.e. [[Complete Metric Spaces (Completeness - Non-Completeness)|complete]])
```
