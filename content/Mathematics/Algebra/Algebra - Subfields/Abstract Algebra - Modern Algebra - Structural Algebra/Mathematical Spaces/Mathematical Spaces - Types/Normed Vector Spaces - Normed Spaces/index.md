###### Normed Vector Spaces - Normed Spaces (𝑉,𝐹,||·||)

```excerpt
- is a type of [[Mathematical Spaces|mathematical space]]
- is a [[Vector Spaces - Linear Spaces|vector space (𝑉,𝐹)]] over the [[Real Numbers System|real]] or [[Complex Numbers System|complex]] numbers on which a [[Norms - Semi-Norms|norm (||·||)]] is defined
- a [[Norms - Semi-Norms|norm (||·||)]]induces a [[Distance Measures／Metrics／Semi-Metrics／Functions|distance metric (𝑑||·||)]] defined as: 𝑑<sub>||·||</sub>(𝑥,𝑦) = ||𝑥-𝑦||
	- thus all <em>normed spaces (𝑉,𝐹,||·||)</em> are special cases of [[Metric Spaces|metric spaces (𝑉,𝑑||·||)]]
```

^excerpt
