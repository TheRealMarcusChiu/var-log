###### Connectedness
````excerpt
- is one of the principal [topological properties](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Topological_properties) that are used to distinguish [[Topological Spaces|topological spaces]]
- a subset of a topological space 𝑋 is a <strong>connected set</strong> if it is a [[Connected Spaces - Disconnected Spaces|connected space]] when viewed as a [[Subspaces|subspace]] of 𝑋
- some related but stronger conditions are [[Path Connectedness|path-connected]], [[Simply Connectedness|simply connected]], and [[N-Connected - Homotopical Connectivity|𝑛-connected]]
	- another related notion is <em>[locally connected](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Locally_connected_space)</em>, which neither implies nor follows from connectedness
````
