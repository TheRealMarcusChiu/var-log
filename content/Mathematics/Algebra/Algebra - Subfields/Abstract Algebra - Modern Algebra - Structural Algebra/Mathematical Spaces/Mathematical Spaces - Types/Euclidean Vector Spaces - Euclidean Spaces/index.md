# Euclidean Space - Types

- ℝ<sup>1</sup> = ℝ is the set of real numbers (i.e., the real line)
- ℝ<sup>2</sup> is called the Euclidean Plane
- ℝ<sup>3</sup> is called the 3D Euclidean Space
