###### c<sub>00</sub> spaces - Space of Eventually Zero Sequences (c<sub>00</sub>)
````excerpt
- is a type of [[Mathematical Spaces|mathematical space]]
- is a [[Subspaces|subspace]] of [[c0 spaces - Space of Null Sequences - Space of Vanishing Sequences|c0 space]] consisting of all sequences which have only finitely many nonzero elements
- the sequence (𝑥<sub>𝑛</sub>) ∊ 𝐹<sup>ℕ</sup> is an <em>eventually zero sequence</em> if and only if there exists an 𝑁∊ℕ such that for all 𝑛≥𝑁, 𝑥<sub>𝑛</sub> = 0
````
^excerpt
