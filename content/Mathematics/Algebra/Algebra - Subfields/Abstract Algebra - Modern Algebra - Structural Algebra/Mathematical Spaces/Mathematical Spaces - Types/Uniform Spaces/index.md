###### Uniform Spaces
````excerpt
- is a type of [[Mathematical Spaces|mathematical space]]
- is a [[Topological Spaces|topological space (𝑋,𝜏)]] with additional [structures](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mathematical_structure) that is used to define <em>[uniform properties](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uniform_property)</em>, such as [[Complete Metric Spaces (Completeness - Non-Completeness)|completeness]], [uniform continuity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uniform_continuity), and [uniform convergence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uniform_convergence)
````
