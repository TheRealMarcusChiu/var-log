###### Limit Points - Accumulation Points - Cluster Points <sup>[[Mathematical Space (Limit Points - Accumulation Points - Cluster Points)|(general definition)]]</sup>

```excerpt
- given a subset (𝑆) of a [[Topological Spaces|topological space (𝑋,𝜏)]] a point 𝑥 in 𝑋 is a <em>limit point</em> of 𝑆 if every [[Topological Spaces (Neighborhoods of a Point／Set)|neighborhood (𝑁𝜏)]] of 𝑥 contains at least one point of 𝑆 different from 𝑥 itself:
	- 𝑥 in 𝑋 is a <em>limit point</em> of 𝑆 ↔ ∀[[Topological Spaces (Neighborhoods of a Point／Set)|𝑁𝜏]](𝑥) ∃𝑦∊𝑆\\{𝑥} : 𝑦∊[[Topological Spaces (Neighborhoods of a Point／Set)|𝑁𝜏]](𝑥)
```
