# Open Cover - Examples
- an open cover of the real line, with respect to the [[Standard／Euclidean／Usual／Natural Topology|Euclidean Topology]], is the set of all [[Real Intervals (Empty／Degenerate／Proper／Bounded／Finite／Half-Bounded／Unbounded／Left／Right／Open／Closed Intervals)|open intervals]] (-𝑛,𝑛), where 𝑛∊ℕ
- an open cover of the open interval (0,1), is the set of all intervals (1/𝑛,1), where 𝑛∊ℕ\\{0}
