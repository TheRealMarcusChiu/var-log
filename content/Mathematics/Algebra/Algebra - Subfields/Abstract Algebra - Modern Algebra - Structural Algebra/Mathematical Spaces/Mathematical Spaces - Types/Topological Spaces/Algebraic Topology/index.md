###### Algebraic topology
````excerpt
- is a branch of mathematics that uses tools from [[Abstract Algebra - Modern Algebra - Structural Algebra|abstract algebra]] to study [[Topological Spaces|topological spaces]]
- the basic goal is to find algebraic invariants that classify topological spaces up to [[Homeomorphic／Homeomorphisms - Topological Isomorphism ／ Topologically Isomorphic - Bicontinuous Function|homeomorphism]], though usually most classify up to homotopy equivalence
````
