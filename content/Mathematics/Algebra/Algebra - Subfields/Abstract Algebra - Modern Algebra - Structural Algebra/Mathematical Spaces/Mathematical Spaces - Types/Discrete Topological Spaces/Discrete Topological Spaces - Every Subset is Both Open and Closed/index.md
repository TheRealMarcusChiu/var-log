Given a [[Discrete Topological Spaces|discrete topological space (𝑋,𝜏)]]:

- the [[Topology|topology (𝜏)]] contains every possible subset of 𝑋 (i.e. 𝜏 = powerset of 𝑋)
- thus every subset 𝑆 of 𝑋 is open because 𝑆 exists in 𝜏
- thus every subset 𝑆 of 𝑋 is also closed because the complement 𝑋\𝑆 is open (i.e. 𝑋\𝑆 exists in 𝜏)
