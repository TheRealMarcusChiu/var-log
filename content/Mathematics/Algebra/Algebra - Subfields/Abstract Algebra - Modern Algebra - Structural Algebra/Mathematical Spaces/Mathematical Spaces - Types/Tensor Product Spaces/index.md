###### Tensor Product Spaces
````excerpt
- is a type of [[Mathematical Spaces|mathematical space]]
- is a way of creating a new, larger vector space by combining two (or more) smaller [[Vector Spaces - Linear Spaces|vector spaces]] (the elements of this new space are called [[Tensors|tensors]])
````
