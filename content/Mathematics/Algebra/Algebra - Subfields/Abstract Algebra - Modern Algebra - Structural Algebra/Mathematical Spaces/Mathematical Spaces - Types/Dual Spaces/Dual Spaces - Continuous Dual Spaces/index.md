###### Dual Spaces - Continuous Dual Spaces (𝑉\*, 𝑉<sup>∨</sup>, or 𝑉')
````excerpt
- is a type of [[Mathematical Spaces|mathematical space]]
- is a [[Dual Spaces|dual space]]
- any [[Topological Vector Spaces (TVS) - Linear Topological Spaces|topological vector spaces (𝑉,𝐹,𝜏)]] has a corresponding <strong><em>dual space (𝑉\*, 𝑉<sup>∨</sup>, or 𝑉')</em></strong> consisting of all continuous [[Linear Functionals - Linear Forms - 1／One-Forms - Covectors|linear forms]]
- when defined for [[Vector Spaces - Linear Spaces|vector spaces]] see [[Dual Spaces - Algebraic Dual Spaces - Dual Vector Spaces|Algebraic Dual Spaces]]
````
