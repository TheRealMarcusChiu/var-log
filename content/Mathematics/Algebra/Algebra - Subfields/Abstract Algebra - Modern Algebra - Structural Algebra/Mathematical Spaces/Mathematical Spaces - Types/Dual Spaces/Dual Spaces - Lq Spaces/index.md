# Lq Spaces - Intuition

If you think of integration as fancy sums, then multiplying a row vector by a column vector in finite dimensions:

- $\sum_{i=1}^n{v_iw_i}$

becomes an integral for 𝑓 in 𝐿<sup>𝑝</sup> and 𝑔 in its dual space 𝐿<sup>𝑞</sup>:

- $\int f(x)g(x)dx$
