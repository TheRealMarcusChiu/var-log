```excerpt
- is a type of [[Mathematical Spaces|mathematical space]]
- the <strong>dual space</strong> of a given space 𝑉 is denoted as 𝑉'
- the <strong>dual space</strong> is the space of all <strong>linear functionals</strong> on the primal space
```
