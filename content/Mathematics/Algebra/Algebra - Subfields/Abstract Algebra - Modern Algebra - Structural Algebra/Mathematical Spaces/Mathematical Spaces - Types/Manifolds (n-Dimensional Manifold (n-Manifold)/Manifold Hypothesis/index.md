###### Manifold Hypothesis

```excerpt
- posits that many high-dimensional data sets that occur in the real world actually lie along low-dimensional latent manifolds inside that high-dimensional space
- as a consequence of the <em>manifold hypothesis</em>, many data sets that appear to initially require many variables to describe can actually be described by a comparatively small number of variables, likened to the [[Local Coordinate System|local coordinate system]] of the underlying manifold
- this hypothesis is rooted in [[Non-Linear／Nonlinear Dimensionality Reduction (NLDR) - Manifold Learning|manifold learning]]
```

^excerpt
