###### Intrinsic Dimension/Dimensionality

- the intrinsic dimension for a data set can be thought of as the number of variables needed in a minimal representation of the data

###### Embedding Dimension/Dimensionality

- the embedding dimension is the dimension of the surrounding Euclidean space that the data/manifold is sitting inside of
