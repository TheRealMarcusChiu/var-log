# Riemannian Manifolds - Riemannian Spaces (𝑀,𝑔)

```excerpt
- is a real, [[Smooth Manifolds|smooth manifold]] in differential geometry
- it's named after the German mathematician Bernhard Riemann
- a Riemannian manifold (𝑀,𝑔) is a real, [[Smooth Manifolds|smooth manifold]] 𝑀 equipped with an [[Inner Products|inner product]] 𝑔<sub>𝑝</sub> on the [tangent space](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tangent_space) 𝑇<sub>𝑝</sub>𝑀 at each point 𝑝
	- the family of inner products 𝑔<sub>𝑝</sub> is called a [[Riemannian Metric - Riemannian Metric Tensor|Riemannian metric]]
```
