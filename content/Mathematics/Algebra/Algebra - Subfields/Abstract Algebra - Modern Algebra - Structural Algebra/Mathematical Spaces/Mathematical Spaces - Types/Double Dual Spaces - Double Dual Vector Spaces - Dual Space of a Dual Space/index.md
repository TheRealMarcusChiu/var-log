###### Double Dual Spaces - Double Dual Vector Spaces
````excerpt
- related to [[Dual Spaces - Algebraic Dual Spaces - Dual Vector Spaces|Dual Vector Spaces]]
- is the space/set of [[Linear Functionals - Linear Forms - 1／One-Forms - Covectors|linear functionals]] on linear functionals on 𝑉
- if 𝑉 is a [[Vector Spaces - Linear Spaces|vector space]] over a [[Fields (Algebraic Structure) - Field Theory|field]] 𝐹, then:
	- the space/set of all [[Linear Functionals - Linear Forms - 1／One-Forms - Covectors|linear functionals]] from 𝑉 to 𝐹 is itself a vector space over 𝐹. This space is called the [[Dual Spaces - Algebraic Dual Spaces - Dual Vector Spaces|dual vector space]] of 𝑉 denoted as 𝑉\*
	- the space/set of all linear functionals from 𝑉\* to 𝐹 is itself a vector space over 𝐹. This space is called the double dual space of 𝑉 denoted as 𝑉\*\*
- 𝑉\*\* is "naturally" [[Morphisms - Homomorphic／Homomorphisms (Isomorphic／Isomorphisms - Monomorphic／Monomorphisms／Injective - Epimorphic／Epimorphisms／Surjective - Endomorphic／Endomorphisms - Automorphic／Automorphisms)|isomorphic]] to 𝑉
````
