###### Quaternion Algebra

```excerpt
- utilizes [[Quaternion Number System]]
- is the subset of 3D [[Geometric Algebra (GA) - Clifford Algebra|geometric algebra (GA)]] and [[Projective Geometric Algebra (PGA)|projective geometric algebra (PGA)]] that performs rotations about axes passing through the origin
- is contained in the GA signature of (3,0,0), which is further contained in the PGA (3,0,1)
```
