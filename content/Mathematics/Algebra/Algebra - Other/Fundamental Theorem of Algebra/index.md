###### Fundamental Theorem of Algebra

```excerpt
- A theorem of equations proved by Carl Friedrich Gauss in 1799
- It states that every polynomial equation of degree 𝑛 with [[Complex Numbers System|complex number]] coefficients has 𝑛 roots, or solutions, in the complex numbers. The roots can have a multiplicity greater than zero
```

^excerpt

# Use Cases

- [[Every n×n matrix has exactly n complex eigenvalues, counted with multiplicity]]
