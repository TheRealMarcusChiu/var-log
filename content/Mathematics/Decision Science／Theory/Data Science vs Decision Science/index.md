- For <strong>[[Decision Science／Theory|Decision Science]]</strong>, the business problem comes first. Analysis follows and is dependent on the question or business decision that needs to be made.
- For <strong>[[Data Science／Scientist／Analytics／Analyst／Infrastructure／Engineering|Data Science]]</strong>, the business problem is not always first. Analysis is done independently and when presented to business they make decisions.
