###### Dynamical Systems Theory (DST)
````excerpt
- is a mathematical framework used to understand how complex systems change over time
- it views systems through a "state space" and uses [[Differential Equations (DE) - Ordinary Differential Equations (ODEs) - Partial Differential Equations (PDEs)|differential equations]] or other rules to model their evolution
- focuses on the interconnectedness of system components, emergent patterns, nonlinear changes (like sudden shifts in behavior), and the role of control parameters in shaping a system's trajectory
````
