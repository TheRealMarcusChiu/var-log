###### BQP (Bounded-Error Quantum Polynomial-Time)
````excerpt
- BQP is a type of [[Complexity Classes|complexity class]] of [[Decision Problems - Optimization Problems|decision problems]] solvable by a [[Quantum Turing Machine (QTM)|quantum Turing machine]] in polynomial time, with an error probability of at most 1/3 for all instances
- BQP is the quantum analog to the complexity class [[BPP (Bounded-Error Probabilistic Polynomial Time)|BPP]]
````
^excerpt
