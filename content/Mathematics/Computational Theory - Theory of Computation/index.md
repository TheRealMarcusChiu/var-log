###### Computational Theory

```excerpt
- the theory of computation is the branch that deals with what [[Problems|problems]] can be solved on a [[Model of Computation - Computational Model|model of computation]], using an [[Algorithms|algorithm]], how efficiently they can be solved, and/or to what degree (e.g., approximate solutions versus precise ones)
- this field is divided into three major branches, which are linked by the question: <font style="color: rgb(122,134,154);"><em>"What are the fundamental capabilities and limitations of computers?"</em></font>
	- [[Automata Theory|automata theory]] and [[Formal Grammar - Formal Language|formal languages]]
	- [[Computability Theory - Recursion Theory|computability theory]]
	- [[Computational Complexity Theory|computational complexity theory]]
```

^excerpt
