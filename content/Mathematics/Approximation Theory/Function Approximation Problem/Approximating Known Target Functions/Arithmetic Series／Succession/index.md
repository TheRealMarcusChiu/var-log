###### Arithmetic Series/Succession

```excerpt
- the name indicates each term is the arithmetic mean of its two neighboring terms
```

^excerpt

# Arithmetic Series - Formula$S_n = \sum_{i=0}^n a_1 + i$$S_n = n \frac{a_1 + a_n}{2}$

where:

- 𝑛 - the number of terms being added
- 𝑎<sub>1</sub> - the first term
- 𝑎<sub>𝑛</sub> - the n<sup>th</sup> term

# Examples

- 𝑆<sub>𝑛</sub> = 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9
