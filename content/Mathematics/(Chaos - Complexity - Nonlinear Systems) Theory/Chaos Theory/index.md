<strong>Chaos Theory</strong>
- is the study of chaos — non-linear dynamical systems whose <strong>apparently random</strong> states of disorder and irregularities <strong>are actually governed by deterministic laws</strong> that are highly sensitive to initial conditions
- a deterministic system can be chaotic (deterministic yet unpredictable)
