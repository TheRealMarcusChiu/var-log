- <strong>[[Chaos Theory]]</strong> seeks an understanding of simple systems that may change in a sudden, unexpected, or irregular way
- <strong>[[Complexity Theory (Complexity Science)|Complexity Theory]]</strong> focuses on complex systems involving numerous interacting parts, which often give rise to unexpected order
- <strong>[[Nonlinear Systems Theory]]</strong> studies how variation can shape the irreversible evolutionary paths of complex systems
