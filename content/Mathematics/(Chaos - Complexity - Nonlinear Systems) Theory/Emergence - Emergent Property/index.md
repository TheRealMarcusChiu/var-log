###### Emergence - Emergent Property
- is a general process where an order is formed out of the local interactions which give rise to the overall organization.
- thus in [[System - Complex System|complex systems]]:
	- we do not search for global rules that govern the whole system
	- we do search for local rules that give rise to an emergent organization
