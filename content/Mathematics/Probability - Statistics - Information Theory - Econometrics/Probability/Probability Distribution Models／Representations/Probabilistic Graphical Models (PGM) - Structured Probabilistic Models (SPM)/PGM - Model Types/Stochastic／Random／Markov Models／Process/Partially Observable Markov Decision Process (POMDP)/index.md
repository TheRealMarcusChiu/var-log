###### Partially Observable Markov Decision Process (POMDP)
````excerpt
- is a [[Markov Decision Process (MDP)|Markov Decision Process]] in which the state of the system is only partially observed
- are known to be [[NP Complete／Completeness Problems|NP Complete]], but recent approximation techniques have made them useful for a variety of applications, such as controlling simple agents or robots
````
