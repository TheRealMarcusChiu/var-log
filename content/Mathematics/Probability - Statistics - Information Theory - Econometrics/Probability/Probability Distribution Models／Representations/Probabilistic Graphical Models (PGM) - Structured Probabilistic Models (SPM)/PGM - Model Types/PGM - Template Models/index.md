###### Template Models
- are used to model distributions that have a recurring structure
- representations for 2 such situations:
	- temporal models - used to model a static/dynamic probabilistic structure over time
		- static temporal models - [[Hidden Markov Models (HMM)|Hidden Markov Models]]
		- dynamic temporal models - [[Dynamic Bayesian Networks (DBN)|Dynamic Bayesian Networks]]
	- scenarios that involve multiple similar entities, each of whose properties is governed by a similar model (e.g. super pixel clusters)
