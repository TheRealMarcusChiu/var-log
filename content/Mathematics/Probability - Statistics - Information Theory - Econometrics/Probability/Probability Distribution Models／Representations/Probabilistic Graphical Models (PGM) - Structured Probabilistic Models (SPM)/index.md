###### Probabilistic Graphical Model (PGM) or Structured Probabilistic Model (SPM)
````excerpt
- is a type of [[Probability Distribution Models／Representations|probability distribution model]] that uses [[Graphical Models|graphical models]] to systematically capture the behavior of a [[Probability Distributions|probability distribution]]
- is used to build models from data that are probabilistic/non-deterministic
- can be used for a wide range of tasks including [[Probability - Generating Random Variable(s) that Simulates a Distribution|simulated probabilities]], making [[Probabilistic Inference|probabilistic inferences]], prediction, anomaly detection, diagnostics, automated insight, reasoning, time series prediction, and decision-making under uncertainty.
````
