###### Non-Parametric Probability Distribution Models

```excerpt
- models [[Probability Distributions|probability distributions]] without [[Statistics - Terminology|parameters]] (i.e. non-parametric functions/models)
- differ from [[Parametric Probability Distribution Models|parametric probability distribution models]] in that the model described with parameters but is instead determined from data
- the term <em>non-parametric</em> is not meant to imply that such models completely lack parameters but that the number and nature of the parameters are flexible and not fixed in advance
```
