<strong>effective sample size</strong> is a notion defined for a sample from a distribution/population when the observations in the sample are <font style="color: rgb(255,153,0);">correlated</font> or <font style="color: rgb(255,153,0);">weighted</font>

### Correlated Observations in Sample

effective sample size = 𝑛<sub>𝑒𝑓𝑓</sub> = 𝑛 / (1 + (𝑛 - 1)𝜌)

where:

- 𝜌 - correlation between observations in sample
- 𝑛 - number of samples
