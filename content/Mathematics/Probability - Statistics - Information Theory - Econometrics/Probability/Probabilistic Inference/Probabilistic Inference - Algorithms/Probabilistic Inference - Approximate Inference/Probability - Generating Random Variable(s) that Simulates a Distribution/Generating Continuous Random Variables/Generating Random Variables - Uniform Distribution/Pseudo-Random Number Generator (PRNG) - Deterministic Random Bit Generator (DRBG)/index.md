A <strong>Pseudo-Random Number Generator (PRNG)</strong>, also known as a <strong>Deterministic Random Bit Generator (DRBG)</strong>, is an algorithm for generating a sequence of numbers whose properties approximate the properties of sequences of random numbers.

PRNGs are central in applications such as:

- [simulations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Simulation) (e.g. for the [[Monte Carlo (MC) Methods／Experiments／Simulations|Monte Carlo method]])
- [electronic games](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electronic_game) (e.g. for [procedural generation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Procedural_generation))
- [[Cryptography & Cryptanalysis|cryptography]]
