###### Product Rule

> [!indent]
> 𝐏(𝐴𝐵) = 𝐏(𝐴|𝐵)𝐏(𝐵)

###### Chain Rule

chain rule is an extension of the product rule above

> [!indent]
> 𝐏(𝑋<sub>1</sub>, ..., 𝑋<sub>𝑛</sub>) = 𝛱<sub>1≤𝑖≤𝑛</sub>𝐏(𝑋<sub>𝑖</sub>|𝑋<sub>𝑖+1</sub>, ..., 𝑋<sub>𝑛</sub>)
