###### Bayes Factor - Likelihood Ratio

- is a likelihood ratio of the marginal likelihood of two competing hypotheses, usually a null and an alternative

# Formula

- Bayes Factor = [[Sensitivity vs Specificity|Sensitivity]] / (1 - [[Sensitivity vs Specificity|Specificity]])
- Bayes Factor = Sensitivity / [[True／False Positive／Negative Sensitivity Recall Hit／Miss／False-Discovery／False-Omission Rate Fall-Out Threat／F1 Score Critical Success Index Accuracy Specificity Precision Predictive-Value|False Positive Rate]]
- Bayes Factor = 𝐏(𝐻𝑌𝑃𝑂𝑇𝐻𝐸𝑆𝐼𝑆=𝑡𝑟𝑢𝑒|𝐴𝐶𝑇𝑈𝐴𝐿=𝑡𝑟𝑢𝑒) / 𝐏(𝐻𝑌𝑃𝑂𝑇𝐻𝐸𝑆𝐼𝑆=𝑡𝑟𝑢𝑒|𝐴𝐶𝑇𝑈𝐴𝐿=𝑓𝑎𝑙𝑠𝑒)
