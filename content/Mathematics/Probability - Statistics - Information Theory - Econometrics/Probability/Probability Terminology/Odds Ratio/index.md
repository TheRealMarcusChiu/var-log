###### Odds Ratio

- is the ratio of [[Probability vs Odds|odds]]

# Determining if an Odds Ratio or Log(Odds Ratio) is Statistically Significant

3 methods:

- <strong>Fisher's Exact Test</strong> - used to calculate the p-value
- <strong>[[Chi-Squared／Square Test|Chi-Square Test]]</strong> - used to calculate the p-value
- <strong>The Wald Test</strong> - used to calculate the p-value and/or confidence interval
