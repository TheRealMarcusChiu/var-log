###### Gambler's Fallacy - Monte Carlo Fallacy - Fallacy of the Maturity of Chances
- a type of [[Cognitive Biases|cognitive bias]] that an:
	- event will occur more likely than normal if the event didn't occur several times in the past
	- event will occur less likely than normal if the event occurred several times in the past
- even though it has been established that the probability of such events does not depend on the past
