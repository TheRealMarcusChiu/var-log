###### Probability Independence
````excerpt
- two [[Probability Spaces (Sample Space - Event Space - Probability Measure)|events]] are <strong>independent</strong> if the outcome or occurrence of the first DOES NOT affect the outcome or occurrence of the second so that the [[Probability|probability]] is NOT changed
- Thus events 𝐴 and 𝐵 are independent iff the following holds:
	- 𝐏(𝐴|𝐵) = 𝐏(𝐴)
	- 𝐏(𝐵|𝐴) = 𝐏(𝐵)
````
^excerpt
