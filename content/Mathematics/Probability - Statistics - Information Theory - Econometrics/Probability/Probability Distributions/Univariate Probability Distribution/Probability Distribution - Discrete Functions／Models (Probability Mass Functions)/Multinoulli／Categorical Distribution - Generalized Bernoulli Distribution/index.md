- <sup>\[𝑥=𝑐𝑙𝑎𝑠𝑠-𝑖]</sup> - is the [Iverson bracket](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iverson_bracket) (i.e. evaluates to 1 when true, otherwise 0)
