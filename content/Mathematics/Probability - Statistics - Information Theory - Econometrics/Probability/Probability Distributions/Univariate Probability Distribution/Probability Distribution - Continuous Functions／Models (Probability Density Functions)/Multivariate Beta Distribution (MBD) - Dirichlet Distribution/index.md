<strong>Multivariate Beta Distribution (MBD)</strong> - <strong>Dirichlet Distribution</strong>

- is a generalization of [[Multivariate Beta Distribution (MBD) - Dirichlet Distribution]] to multiple variables

### Probability Density Function

- 𝑓(𝑥<sub>1</sub>, ..., 𝑥<sub>𝑘</sub>, 𝛼<sub>1</sub>, ..., 𝛼<sub>𝑘</sub>) = \[1/𝐵(𝛼<sub>1</sub>, ..., 𝛼<sub>𝑘</sub>)] 𝛱<sub>1≤𝑖≤𝑘</sub>\[𝑥<sub>𝑖</sub><sup>(𝛼<sub>𝑖</sub> - 1)</sup>]

where:

- 𝐵(𝛼<sub>1</sub>, ..., 𝛼<sub>𝑘</sub>) - [[Multivariate Beta Function]]
