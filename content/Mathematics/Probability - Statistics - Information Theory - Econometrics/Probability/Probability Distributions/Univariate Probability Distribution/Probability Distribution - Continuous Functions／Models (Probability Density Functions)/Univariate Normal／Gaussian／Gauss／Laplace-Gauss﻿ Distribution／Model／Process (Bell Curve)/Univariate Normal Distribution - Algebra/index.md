given 2 independent random normal variables:
- 𝑋 \~ 𝑁(𝑢<sub>𝑥</sub>, 𝜎<sub>𝑥</sub><sup>2</sup>)
- 𝑌 \~ 𝑁(𝑢<sub>𝑦</sub>, 𝜎<sub>𝑦</sub><sup>2</sup>)

and constants:
- 𝑎
- 𝑏

then
- 𝑎𝑋 \~ 𝑁(𝑎𝑢<sub>𝑥</sub>, 𝑎<sup>2</sup>𝜎<sub>𝑥</sub><sup>2</sup>)
- 𝑋 + 𝑏 \~ 𝑁(𝑢<sub>𝑥</sub> + 𝑏, 𝜎<sub>𝑥</sub><sup>2</sup>)
- 𝑋 + 𝑌 \~ 𝑁(𝑢<sub>𝑥</sub> + 𝑢<sub>𝑦</sub>, 𝜎<sub>𝑥</sub><sup>2</sup> + 𝜎<sub>𝑦</sub><sup>2</sup>)
- 𝑋 − 𝑌 \~ 𝑁(𝑢<sub>𝑥</sub> − 𝑢<sub>𝑦</sub>, 𝜎<sub>𝑥</sub><sup>2</sup> + 𝜎<sub>𝑦</sub><sup>2</sup>)
