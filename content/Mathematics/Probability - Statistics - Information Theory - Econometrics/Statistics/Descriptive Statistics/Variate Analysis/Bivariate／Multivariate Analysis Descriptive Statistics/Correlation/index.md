###### Correlation

```excerpt
- is the degree to which one variable moves in line with another variable
- is a type of bivariate descriptive statistic that measures the strength of the [[Causality／Causation vs Dependence vs Correlation vs Relationships vs Association vs Laws|relationship/association]] between 2 variables
- correlation means that they move together (positive correlation indicates increasing and decreasing together, negative correlation means they move in the opposite direction). linear correlation is more specific still; then they move in proportion, not just in the same (or opposite) direction
- there is no independent and no dependent variable in a correlation
- see also: [[Causality／Causation vs Dependence vs Correlation vs Relationships vs Association vs Laws|Causality/Causation vs Dependence vs Correlation vs Relationships vs Association vs Laws]]
```
