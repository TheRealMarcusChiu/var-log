###### Interquartile Range (IQR)

```excerpt
- is defined as the difference between the third [[Quartiles (4-Quantile)|quartile]] (𝑄<sub>3</sub>) and the first [[Quartiles (4-Quantile)|quartile]] (𝑄<sub>1</sub>)
	- 𝐼𝑄𝑅 = 𝑄<sub>3</sub>− 𝑄<sub>1</sub>
```
