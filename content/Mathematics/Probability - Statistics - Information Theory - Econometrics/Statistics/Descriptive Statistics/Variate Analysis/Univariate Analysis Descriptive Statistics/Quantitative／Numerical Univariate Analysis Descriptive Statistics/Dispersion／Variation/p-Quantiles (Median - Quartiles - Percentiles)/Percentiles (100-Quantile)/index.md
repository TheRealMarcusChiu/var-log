````excerpt
- is a type of [[p-Quantiles (Median - Quartiles - Percentiles)|quantile]] which divides the number of data points into 100 "equal" parts, or percentiles
- is the value such that 𝑝 percent of observations fall at or below it
- [[Median (2-Quantile - 50th Percentile)|median]] is the 50th percentile
````
