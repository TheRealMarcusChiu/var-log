###### Median Deviation
````excerpt
- is a type of [[Central Tendency of Deviation|central tendency of deviation]]
- is the middle distance between each data point and the mean
- is the [[Median (2-Quantile - 50th Percentile)|median]] of the [[Distance Measures／Metrics／Semi-Metrics／Functions|distances]] between each data point and its [[Arithmetic Mean - Average - Location - Expected Value - Expectation Operator|mean]]
````
