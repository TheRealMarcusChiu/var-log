###### Mode Deviation
````excerpt
- is a type of [[Central Tendency of Deviation|central tendency of deviation]]
- is the most occurring distance between each data point and the mean
- is the [[Mode|mode]] of [[Distance Measures／Metrics／Semi-Metrics／Functions|distances]] between each data point and the [[Arithmetic Mean - Average - Location - Expected Value - Expectation Operator|mean]]
````
