````excerpt
- <strong>[[Descriptive Statistics|descriptive statistic]]</strong> (or just <strong>statistic</strong>) (in the [count noun](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Count_noun) sense) is a [summary statistic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Summary_statistic) that quantitatively describes or summarizes features of a collection of information
- <strong>[[Descriptive Statistics|descriptive statistics]]</strong> (in the [mass noun](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mass_noun) sense) is the process of using and analyzing those statistics

<strong>Descriptive Statistics</strong>
- is distinguished from [[Inferential Statistics|inferential statistics]] by its aim to summarize a sample of data, rather than using it to learn about the population that the sample of data is thought to represent
- is often associated with [[Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)]]
````
