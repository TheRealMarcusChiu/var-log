###### Significance Level (𝛼)
- is the [[Probability|probability]] of (REJECTING the [[Inferential Statistical Hypothesis Testing|null hypothesis]] when it is true)
- is the probability of (Type I Error)
- for example, a significance level of 𝛼=0.05 indicates a 5% risk of concluding that a difference exists when there is no actual difference

###### Confidence Level (1 - 𝛼) or Coverage Probability
- is the [probability](http://confluence.marcuschiu.com/display/NOT/Probability) of ACCEPTING the [[Inferential Statistical Hypothesis Testing|null hypothesis]] when it is true
