### Example 1-Tailed & 2-Tailed CIs for Sample Mean 𝑋̅

###### 2-Tailed Confidence Interval

![[Mathematics/Probability - Statistics - Information Theory - Econometrics/Statistics/Inferential Statistics/Interval Estimation/Confidence Interval (CI)/CI - 1-Tailed & 2-Tailed/two-sided-confidence-interval.png|350]]

2-Sided CI

- 𝑧-distribution - \[ 𝑋̅ - 𝑧<sub>𝛼/2</sub>𝑆𝐸(𝑋̅), 𝑋̅ + 𝑧<sub>𝛼/2</sub>𝑆𝐸(𝑋̅) ]
- 𝑡-distribution - \[ 𝑋̅ - 𝑡<sub>𝛼/2</sub>𝑆𝐸(𝑋̅),  𝑋̅ + 𝑡<sub>𝛼/2</sub>𝑆𝐸(𝑋̅) ]

1-Sided Lower CI

- 𝑧-distribution - \[ -∞, 𝑋̅ + 𝑧<sub>𝛼</sub>𝑆𝐸(𝑋̅) ]
- 𝑡-distribution - \[ -∞,  𝑋̅ + 𝑡<sub>𝛼</sub>𝑆𝐸(𝑋̅) ]

1-Sided Upper CI

- 𝑧-distribution - \[ 𝑋̅ - 𝑧<sub>𝛼</sub>𝑆𝐸(𝑋̅), ∞ ]
- 𝑡-distribution - \[ 𝑋̅ - 𝑡<sub>𝛼</sub>𝑆𝐸(𝑋̅), ∞ ]
