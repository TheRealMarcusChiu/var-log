###### Effect
````excerpt
- is the difference between the [[Statistics - Terminology|true population parameter]] and the [[Inferential Statistical Hypothesis Testing|null hypothesis]] value
- is also known as the population effect or the difference (e.g. the mean difference between the health outcome for a treatment group and a control group is the effect)
````
