### Whitney's Theorem
<span style="white-space: pre-wrap"><code>κ(G) ≤ λ(G) ≤ δ(G)</code></span>

where:
- <em><strong>δ(G</strong></em><em><strong>)</strong></em> - denotes the minimum degree of graph G
- <em><strong>λ(G)</strong></em> - denotes the [[Graph Connectivity (edge-connectivity - node connectivity)|edge-connectivity]] of graph G
- <em><strong>κ(G)</strong></em> - denotes the [[Graph Connectivity (edge-connectivity - node connectivity)|node-connectivity]] of graph G
