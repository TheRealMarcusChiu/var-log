<strong>Branch & Bound (B&B) Search</strong>
- is a general method to solve [[Integer／Discrete Linear Programming／Optimization (ILP)|discrete optimization]] problems
- it explores <em>branches</em> of this tree, which represent subsets of the solution set. Before enumerating the candidate solutions of a branch, the branch is checked against upper and lower estimated <em>bounds</em> on the optimal solution and is discarded if it cannot produce a better solution than the best one found so far by the algorithm
