<strong>Moral Graph </strong>is the [[Hypergraphs - Primal Graphs - Dual Graphs - Cluster Graphs - Cluster Trees - Factor Graphs|primal graph]] of a [[Bayesian Networks (BN)|Bayesian Network]] / [[Graph Theory - Terminology|Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG)]]

The moral graph can also be obtained from the directed graph 𝐆 by a process called moralization

###### Moralization Steps

1. connecting all the parents of each child node
2. making all directed arcs undirected
