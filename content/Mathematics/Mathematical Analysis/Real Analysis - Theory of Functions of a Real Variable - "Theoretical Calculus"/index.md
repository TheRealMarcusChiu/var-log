###### Real Analysis - Theory of Functions of a Real Variable - "Theoretical Calculus"
````excerpt
- is a branch of [[Mathematical Analysis|mathematical analysis]] that studies the behavior of [[Real Numbers System|real numbers]], sequences and series of real numbers, and [[Real-Valued Function (Transformations／Operations／Operators／Mappings／Maps／Functions／Morphisms)|real functions]]
- In particular, it deals with the analytic properties of real functions and sequences, including convergence and limits of sequences of real numbers, the calculus of the real numbers, and continuity, smoothness, and related properties of real-valued functions
````
