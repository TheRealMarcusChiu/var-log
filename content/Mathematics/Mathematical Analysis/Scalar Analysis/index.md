###### Scalar Analysis
````excerpt
- is a branch of [[Mathematical Analysis|mathematical analysis]] dealing with values related to scale as opposed to the direction
- values such as temperature are scalar because they describe the magnitude of a value without regard to direction, force, or displacement that value may or may not have
````
