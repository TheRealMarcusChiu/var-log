###### Complex Analysis - Theory of Functions of a Complex Variable

```excerpt
- is the branch of [[Mathematical Analysis|mathematical analysis]] that investigates [[Complex-Valued Function (Transformations／Operations／Operators／Mappings／Maps／Functions／Morphisms)|complex-valued functions]]
- is particularly concerned with the analytic functions of complex variables (or, more generally, meromorphic functions). Because the separate real and imaginary parts of any analytic function must satisfy Laplace's equation, complex analysis is widely applicable to two-dimensional problems in physics
```
