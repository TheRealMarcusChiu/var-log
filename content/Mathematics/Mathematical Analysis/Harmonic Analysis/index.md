###### Harmonic Analysis
````excerpt
- is a branch of [[Mathematical Analysis|mathematical analysis]] concerned with the representation of functions and signals as the superposition of basic waves. This includes the study of the notions of [[Fourier Series|Fourier series]] and [[Fourier Transform - Inverse Fourier Transform|Fourier transforms]] (Fourier analysis), and their generalizations
- has applications in areas as diverse as music theory, [[Number Theory|number theory]], representation theory, signal processing, [[Quantum Physics - Quantum Mechanics|quantum mechanics]], tidal analysis, and neuroscience
````
