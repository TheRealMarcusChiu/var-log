###### Spherical Harmonics
````excerpt
- are a set of special mathematical functions defined on the surface of a sphere
````
^excerpt

# Intuition

Think of spherical harmonics like the spherical version of sine and cosine waves.
- On a line → you use [[Sine Function|sine]]/[[Cosine Function|cosine]] ([[Fourier Series|Fourier series]])
- On a sphere → you use <em>spherical harmonics</em>

They let you break down complicated patterns on a sphere into simpler, smooth “wave-like” components
