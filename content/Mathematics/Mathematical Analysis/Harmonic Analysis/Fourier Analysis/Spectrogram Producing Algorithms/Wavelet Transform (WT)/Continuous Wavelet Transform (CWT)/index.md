###### Continuous Wavelet Transform (CWT)
````excerpt
- is a type of [[Wavelet Transform (WT)|wavelet transform]]
- is used to analyze continuous signals in time ([[Discrete Wavelet Transform (DWT)|DWT]] is the finite version of CWT that works on discrete signals)
````
