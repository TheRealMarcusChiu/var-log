###### Stochastic Calculus

```excerpt
- is a branch of [[Mathematics|mathematics]] that operates on [[Stochastic／Random／Markov Models／Process|stochastic processes]]
- it allows a consistent theory of integration to be defined for [integrals](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Integrals) of stochastic processes with respect to stochastic processes
- this field was created and started by the Japanese mathematician [Kiyosi Itô](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kiyosi_It%C3%B4) during World War II
```
