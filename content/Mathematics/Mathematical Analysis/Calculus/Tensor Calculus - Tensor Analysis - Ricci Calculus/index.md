###### Tensor Calculus - Tensor Analysis - Ricci Calculus
````excerpt
- is an extension of [[Vector Calculus - Vector Analysis|vector calculus]] to [[Tensor-Valued Functions - Tensor Fields|tensor fields]]
- is an extension of [[Tensor Algebra|tensor algebra]] (that incorporates [[Differential Calculus|differentiation]] and [[Integral／Integration／Integrable Calculus|integration]])
- developed by Gregorio Ricci-Curbastro and his student Tullio Levi-Civita, it was used by Albert Einstein to develop his [[Theory of Relativity (General／Special Relativity)|general theory of relativity]]
````
