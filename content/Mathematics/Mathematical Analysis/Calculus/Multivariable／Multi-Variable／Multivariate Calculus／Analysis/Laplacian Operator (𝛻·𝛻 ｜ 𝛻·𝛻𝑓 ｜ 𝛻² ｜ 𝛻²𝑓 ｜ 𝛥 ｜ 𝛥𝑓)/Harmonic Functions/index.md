###### Harmonic Functions
````excerpt
- is a [[Scalar-Valued Functions - Scalar Fields|scalar-valued function]] 𝑓(𝑥<sub>1</sub>, ..., 𝑥<sub>𝑛</sub>) whose [[Laplacian Operator (𝛻·𝛻 ｜ 𝛻·𝛻𝑓 ｜ 𝛻² ｜ 𝛻²𝑓 ｜ 𝛥 ｜ 𝛥𝑓)|Laplacian]] is equal to zero:
	- $𝛥f = \sum_i^n \frac{d^2f}{dx_i^2} = 0$
- intuitively, given 𝑓 and a point 𝑥; the average of all points within a distance/radius from 𝑥 is also 𝑥
````
