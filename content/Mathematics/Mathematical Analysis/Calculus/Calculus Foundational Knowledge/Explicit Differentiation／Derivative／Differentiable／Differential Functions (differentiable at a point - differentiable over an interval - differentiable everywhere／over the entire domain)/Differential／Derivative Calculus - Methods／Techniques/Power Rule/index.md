###### Power Rule

```excerpt
- $\frac{d}{dx}x^n = nx^{n-1}$
```

^excerpt

# Power Rule - Intuition

- $\frac{df}{dx} = \frac{\text{amount of change in output}}{\text{amount of change in input}}$
- $\frac{df}{dx} = \frac{f(x + dx) - f(x)}{dx}$
- $\frac{df}{dx} = \frac{(x + dx)^2 - x^2}{dx}$
- $\frac{df}{dx} = \frac{x^2 + 2xdx + dx^2 - x^2}{dx}$
- $\frac{df}{dx} = \frac{2xdx + dx^2}{dx}$
- $\frac{df}{dx} = 2x + dx$
- $\frac{df}{dx} = 2x$
