Find the derivative of the following:
- 𝑦 = 𝑙𝑛(𝑥)

Rewrite it in a different way:
- 𝑒<sup>𝑦</sup> = 𝑥

Take its derivative:
- 𝑒<sup>𝑦</sup>𝑑𝑦 = 𝑑𝑥
- 𝑑𝑦/𝑑𝑥 = 1/𝑒<sup>𝑦</sup>
- 𝑑𝑦/𝑑𝑥 = 1/𝑥
