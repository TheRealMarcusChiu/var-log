###### Implicit Differentiation/Derivative/Differentiable/Differential Functions

```excerpt
- In implicit differentiation, we differentiate each side of an equation with two variables (usually 𝑥 and 𝑦) by treating one of the variables as a function of the other.
```

^excerpt

# Examples

> [!expand]- Click here to expand...
>
> - $f(x,y) = x^2 + y + 3xy$
> - $\frac{d[f(x,y)]}{dx} = 2x + 1 + 3x + 3y$
