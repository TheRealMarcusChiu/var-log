###### Numerical Aperture (NA)

```excerpt
- is defined as being equal to 𝑛 𝑠𝑖𝑛 𝜃, where:
	- 𝑛 is the refractive index of the medium between the objective lens and the object (n≅1 for air)
	- 𝜃 is half the angular aperture (or acceptance angle of image-forming rays) of the objective lens
```
