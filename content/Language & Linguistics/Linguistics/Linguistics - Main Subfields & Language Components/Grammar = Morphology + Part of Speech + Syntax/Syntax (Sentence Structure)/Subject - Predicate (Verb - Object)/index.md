every complete <strong>sentence</strong> contains two parts:

- <strong>subject </strong>is what (or whom) the sentence is about. is the transceiver of the verb
- <strong>predicate </strong>tells something about the subject. is the rest of the sentence without the subject
  - <strong>verb</strong> is the action
  - <strong>object</strong> is the receiver of the verb

### Examples

<font style="color: rgb(128,0,0);">Subject</font> {<font style="color: rgb(0,128,0);">Verb</font> <font style="color: rgb(51,102,255);">Object</font>} - {} encloses the predicate

- <font style="color: rgb(128,0,0);">Judy</font> {<font style="color: rgb(51,153,102);">runs</font> <font style="color: rgb(51,102,255);">home</font>}
- <font style="color: rgb(128,0,0);">Judy and her dog</font> {<font style="color: rgb(51,153,102);">runs</font> on the <font style="color: rgb(51,102,255);">beach</font> every morning}
