###### Psycholinguistics - Psychology of Language

- is the study of the mental aspects of language and speech
- it is primarily concerned with the ways in which language is represented and processed in the brain
- is the study of the interrelation between [[Linguistics|linguistics]] and [[Psychology|psychology]]
- is part of the field of cognitive science
