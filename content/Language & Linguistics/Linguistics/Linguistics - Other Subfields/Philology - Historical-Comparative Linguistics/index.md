- <strong>Philology</strong> - the branch of knowledge that deals with the structure, historical development, and relationships of a language or languages
- <strong>Historical-Comparative Linguistics</strong> - branch of historical linguistics that is concerned with comparing languages to establish their historical relatedness

# Terminology

- <strong>glottochronology</strong> - the use of statistical data to date the divergence of languages from their common sources
- <strong>proto-language</strong> - proto- meaning first. an idea that all existing languages come from a common "proto-language"
