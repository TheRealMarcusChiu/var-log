- <strong>portmanteau</strong> - a word blending the sounds and combining the meanings of two others, for example <em>motel</em> (from ‘motor’ and ‘hotel’) or <em>brunch</em> (from ‘breakfast’ and ‘lunch’)
- <strong>contraction</strong> - the process of becoming smaller, for example <em>aren't</em> (from 'are' and 'not')
