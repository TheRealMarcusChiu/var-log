###### Design

- to prepare the plans for (a work to be executed), especially to plan the form and structure of
- a plan produced to show the look and functionality before it is made

###### Good Design

1. separating into things that can be composed
2. composing them to solve a problem
3. iterative

# What Can Be Taken Appart

> [!expand-ui]- requirements
> separate from:
>
> - wants and needs
> - knowns and unknowns
> - domain-side and solution-side
> - cause and symptom
> - unstated

> [!expand-ui]- time/order/flow
>
> - queues
> - idempotency
> - commutative
> - transactions

> [!expand-ui]- place/participants
> "All problems in computer science can be solved by another level of indirection"
>
> Also authors:
>
> - independent development etc

> [!expand-ui]- information/mechanism
>
> - information example - set of logged-in users
> - mechanism example - set collection class/construct

> [!expand-ui]- take apart solutions
>
> - benefits
> - tradeoffs
> - costs
> - problem fit
