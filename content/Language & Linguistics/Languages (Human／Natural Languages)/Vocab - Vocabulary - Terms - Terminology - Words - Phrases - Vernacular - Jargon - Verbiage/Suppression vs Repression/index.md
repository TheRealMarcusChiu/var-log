- <font style="color: rgb(77,89,104);"><strong>repression</strong> refers to the </font><font style="color: rgb(77,89,104);">SUBCONSCIOUSLY</font><font style="color: rgb(77,89,104);"> act of not acknowledging or acting upon one’s feelings, thoughts, and wants</font>
