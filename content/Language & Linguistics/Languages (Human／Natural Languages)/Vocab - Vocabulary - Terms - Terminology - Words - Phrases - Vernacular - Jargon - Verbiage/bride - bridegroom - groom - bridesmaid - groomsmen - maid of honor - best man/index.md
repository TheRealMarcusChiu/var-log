### <strong>Wedding Day Terms</strong>
- <strong>bride</strong> - a woman on her wedding day or just before and after the event
- <strong> bridegroom</strong> or<strong> groom </strong>- a man on his wedding day or just before and after the event
- <strong>bridesmaid</strong> - a female friend attending bride at wedding
- <strong>groomsmen</strong> - a male friend attending bridegroom at wedding
- <strong>maid of honor</strong> - an unmarried woman acting as principal bridesmaid at a wedding
- <strong>matron of honor</strong>  - a married woman acting as principal bridesmaid at a wedding
- <strong>best man</strong> - a male friend or relative chosen by a bridegroom to assist him at his wedding
