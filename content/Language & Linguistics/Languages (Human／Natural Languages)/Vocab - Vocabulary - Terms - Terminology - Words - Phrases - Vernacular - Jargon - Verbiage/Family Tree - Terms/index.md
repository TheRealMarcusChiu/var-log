# Terms

- <strong>full-sibling </strong>- a person who shares the same set of biological parents
- <strong>half-sibling </strong>- siblings having a common mother or father but not having the same biological father or mother respectively
  - <strong>uterine sibling</strong> - a half sibling with the same mother
  - <strong>agnate</strong> or <strong>consanguine</strong> - a half sibling with the same father
- <strong>step-</strong> - is someone who is not related to you by blood
