<em><font style="color: rgb(128,128,128);">"A <strong>worried</strong> person sees a problem, and a <strong>concerned</strong> person solves a problem"</font></em>

<em><font style="color: rgb(128,128,128);">"<em>Anxiety does not empty tomorrow of its sorrows, but only empties today of its strength" ~ Charles Spurgeon</em></font></em>

<strong>concern</strong> - a matter of (past and/or future) interest or importance to someone

<strong>worry</strong> - to allow one's mind to dwell on (past and/or future) difficulty or troubles
