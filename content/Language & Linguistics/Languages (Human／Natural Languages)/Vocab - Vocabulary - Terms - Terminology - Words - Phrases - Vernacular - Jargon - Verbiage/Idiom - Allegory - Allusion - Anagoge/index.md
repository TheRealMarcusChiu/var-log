- <strong>idiom</strong> is a group of words established by usage as having a meaning not deducible from those of the individual words (e.g., <em>rain cats and dogs</em>, <em>see the light</em>)
- <strong>allegory</strong> - a (story, poem, or picture) that can be interpreted to reveal a hidden meaning (typically a moral or political meaning)
- <strong>allusion</strong> - an indirect expression to something
- <strong>anagoge</strong> - interpretation of a word, passage, or text (as of Scripture or poetry) that finds beyond the literal, allegorical, and moral senses a fourth and ultimate spiritual or mystical sense
