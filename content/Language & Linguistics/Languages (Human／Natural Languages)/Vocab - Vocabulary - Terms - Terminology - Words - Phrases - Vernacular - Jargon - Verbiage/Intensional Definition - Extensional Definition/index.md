- <strong>intensional definition</strong> gives the meaning of a term by specifying necessary and sufficient conditions for when the term should be used (e.g. an intensional definition of the word "bachelor" is "unmarried man")
- <strong>extensional definition</strong> gives the meaning of a term by listing everything that falls under that definition (e.g. an extensional definition of <em>bachelor</em> would be a listing of all the unmarried men in the world)
