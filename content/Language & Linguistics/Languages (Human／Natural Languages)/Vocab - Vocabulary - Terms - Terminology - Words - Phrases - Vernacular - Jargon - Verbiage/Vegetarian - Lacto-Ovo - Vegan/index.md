### <strong>Vegetarian Types</strong>
- <strong>lacto-ovo vegetarians</strong>: vegetarians who avoid all animal flesh, but do consume dairy and egg products
- <strong>lacto vegetarians</strong>: vegetarians who avoid animal flesh and eggs, but do consume dairy products
- <strong>ovo vegetarians</strong>: vegetarians who avoid all animal products except eggs
- <strong>vegans</strong>: vegetarians who avoid all animal and animal-derived product
