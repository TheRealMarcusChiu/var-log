- <strong>disposition</strong>
  - the (predominant or prevailing) tendency of one's <strong>mental</strong> and <strong>emotional</strong> (outlook or mood)
  - similar to temperament
- <strong>character</strong>
  - the aggregate of "non-physical/internal" (features/traits/characteristics) which forms the individual nature of a person
  - set of morals and beliefs that defines how we treat or behave with others and ourselves
  - is a collection of characteristic. characteristics are often innate however not immutable
- <strong>personality</strong>
  - has to do with how other people views a person's character
  - the "visible" aspect of one's character with regard to another
  - the range of distinctive personal qualities and traits of an individual
- <strong>temperament</strong>
  - person's nature, especially as it permanently affects their behavior
  - similar to disposition
