###### mittere

meaning "to let go or "send."

###### <strong>dis-</strong>

- similar to Greek's [[Greek Vocabulary|un-]]
- "lack of, not" (as in dishonest)
- "opposite of, do the opposite of" (as in disallow)
- "apart, away" (as in discard)

###### anno domini

"in the year of the Lord"

###### nātālis

1. a birthday
2. any anniversary, a commemorative festival
3. the day of the foundation of Rome

###### fugere

to flee
