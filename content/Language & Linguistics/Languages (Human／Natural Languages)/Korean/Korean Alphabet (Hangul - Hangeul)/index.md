<strong>Hangul</strong> or <strong>Hangeul</strong> (Korean: 한글)

```excerpt
- is the alphabet (or alphabetic syllabary) used for the [[Korean]] language
- it was made by Sejong the Great, the 4th King of the Joseon Dynasty in 1443, and it is still used
```
