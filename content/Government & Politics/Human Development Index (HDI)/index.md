###### Human Development Index (HDI)

```excerpt
- is a statistical tool that measures a country's overall achievement in its social and economic dimensions
- combines several indicators of health, education, and wealth to measure the average achievements in a country in three basic dimensions of human development:
	- a long and healthy life
	- access to knowledge
	- a decent standard of living
```
