###### Blame!
````excerpt
- Blame! is a Japanese science fiction manga series written and illustrated by Tsutomu Nihei.
- It was published by Kodansha in the seinen manga magazine Monthly Afternoon from 1997 to 2003, with its chapters collected in ten tankōbon volumes.
````
