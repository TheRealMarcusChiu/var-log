###### The Life of Our Lord

```excerpt
- is a book about the life of Jesus of Nazareth written by English novelist Charles Dickens, for his young children, between 1846 and 1849, at about the time that he was writing David Copperfield.
- was published in 1934, 64 years after Dickens's death
```
