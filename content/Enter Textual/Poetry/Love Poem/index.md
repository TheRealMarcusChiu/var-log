I hear a piano sing when I see your eyes,
and violins play when I see you smile.

My heartbeat becomes a bass
when you pull me in your embrace.

You may not find that song,
but I've loved you for so long.

Until the stars hide the moon,
I won't say a thing for the sake of you.

In the few months I have known you,
my blood's become a brighter hue.

You paint my face with blossoms,
the ink of your name at the bottom.

You may not find that work of art,
but you have the core of my heart.

Until gravity divorces the ground,
I won't say a thing to keep us bound.

I need no mask on with you,
I come out of my shell anew.

Thunders of applause fill my chest
when you make me feel my best.

You may not see that show,
but I adore you more than you know.

Until the world turns on its head,
I won't say a thing to just stay your friend.

I just said many things I can't take back,
but honestly I'd trade nothing for that.

Either way, I'll spend the rest of my life
loving you for all you are inside.
